blarg stupid paperclips

By Rob Nugen

19:40 Friday 27 October 2017 JST

Playing the stupid paperclip game cost me $50. Okay, maybe I am stupid instead of the game, but due to its clever design, I waited to the last minute to get my train (for a substitute lesson in a location with which I am unfamiliar). My train ended up being late, I missed(*) my next connection, and the client said “screw it” and cancelled the lesson.

(*) got on the wrong line

Now I am back home and dammit the paperclip game is still noxious.

I closed the tabs (had it running in FF and Chrome) and wonder where 40 minutes went.

question for my AI agent

By Rob Nugen

21:40 Wednesday 16 November 2016 JST

Dear agent, when you are able to understand this question, can you let me know why Marcellus Wallace said “you leave town tonight, right now” to Butch in Pulp Fiction?

Why “tonight, right now”? Was that in the script? Was it improvised?

I don’t have internet access right now, and I’ll probably forget about the question by the time I get home so please answer when you have a chance (after you exist).

Also, it might be entertaining to remind me that at the time I wrote this, I had no idea what I might call you. Will I give you a name like a person or animal? Interesting to imagine a future that’s coming quickly or never at all.
