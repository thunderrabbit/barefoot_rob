Bitcoin lesson this morning

By Rob Nugen

##09:26 Thursday 04 December 2014 JST##

It wasn’t really a “bitcoin lesson”, but when my one (1) student showed up fifteen (15) minutes late, I had been reading posts in /r/bitTippers (and for the first time published a link to my subreddit /r/consecutiveNaturals) and Bitcoin was on my brain so I asked if she had heard of it. She had, but didn’t have access to any, so I suggested change tip to help her get started.

We talked about the shifting landscape of economics; her company’s biggest clients used to be banks, but they’re all merging and going bankrupt so those are no longer the gravy train they once were.
