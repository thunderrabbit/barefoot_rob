three lessons at Toshiba

By Rob Nugen

##18:43 Tuesday 11 November 2014 JST##

Another first lesson today: ‘twas the first time I taught at Toshiba science museum near Lazona in Kawasaki.

I taught three lessons to three individuals, all of whom wore the same uniform, so I’m not sure I could readily identify them in a lineup, but the lessons were fun and I hope I will get to go back after tomorrow, when I’m scheduled to be there for three hours.

The lessons ran from 4pm and they ended promptly at 6pm so I made it in plenty of time to M&M lesson tonight. I’m at Mini Stop having my customary vanilla ice cream cone.
