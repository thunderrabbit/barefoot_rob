yippee!!

By Rob Nugen

12:25am Monday 12 February 2001

In about 24 hours we will be on a 747 in Los Angeles going, "oh my god! We're on a seven forty seven!!!"

06 Jul 2000, 00:00

keyboard!!

By Rob Nugen

1:10pm Thursday 06 July 2000

Wow!!!

This is pretty frikkin’ fly! I just received my stowaway keyboard for my Handspring Visor! I can now write journal entries so crazy super fast anywhere I go! It fold into fourths and fits into my pocket!

It’s a bit weird to have the keyboard slightly flexing when I hit keys on the near the ends of the keyboard, like backspace and shift, but I totally love it!

For example, whereas it would normally have taken me like 15 minutes to type all this text, it’s only taken me about 2 or 3.

Sweet!!

25 Apr 2000, 00:00

Yay!!!

By Rob Nugen

6:50pm CDT Tuesday 25 April 2000

We have a dial tone at home!!! I have just moved all my journal entries online from my computer. Look for names that start with PP up above.

From our house, I called Wende on her cell phone.

“Hey Wende, do you hear that noise?” I was walking on the squeaky part of our floor between our bedroom and bathroom.

“Yeah, what is it?”

“Guess.”

“Is it you farting?”

“Nope.”

“I don’t know.”

I could tell she wasn’t in the mood to guess. “It’s the squeak by our bathroom!!”

“Oh.” she sounded disappointed. Then, “How are you calling from our house??”

Thanks to Mike and Edna; whatever they did worked!!
