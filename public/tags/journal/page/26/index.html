By Rob Nugen

Catching Up

Thursday 10 September 1998

Yes, I haven't written in a while. Sigh.. Sometimes I feel so bogged down and I have so much fun stuff to write!! Tulsa Rally was last weekend, dinner at Niko Niko's was last night, and I've had lots of anti-fun with my scanner not working!

But I'm here on campus at UH again; just ate lunch and said HI to Nikki and Sara and am about to go to Scholars' Community where I used to work on campus. .. .. I wonder if Tina is still workin' there. I assume so. :-) Yay!

I just had a theological conversation with Nikki, and the following questions came to my mind:

  • What do you think happens after we die?
  • What is sin?
  • Do you believe in heaven?
  • Is God male or female or neither?
  • Does God ever become angry?
  • What is the most important thing in life?
  • What are emotions?
  • Is there life on other planets?
  • Do mosquitoes have souls?

Maybe I'll address these in my Gravity section. mail me your answers!
