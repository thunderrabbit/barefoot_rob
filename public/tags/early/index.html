Mondays are early days

By Rob Nugen

14:25 Monday 15 October 2018 JST

On Mondays for the past year or more, I have been waking up at 5am on Mondays to work at IL for an hour from 8am to 9am. The days of visiting those fish auctions are over; one of the bugs/features of Toyosu location is tourists are not allowed in the same room as the auctions. I don’t really care one way or another, but I imagine it’s somehow better to more easily keep the place cleaner.
