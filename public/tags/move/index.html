moved again

By Rob Nugen

Okay, I’m moving my site again. Even though it was never totally moved in the first place. However, this new ISP webquarry.com seems like it will be a much happier place for my site to live. I’m messing with a lot of perl stuff, and the other ISP just wasn’t conducive (sp) to easy perl writing. I asked them if I could have access to the error output of my perl scripts and the answer I got was, “if you have an error in your script, you just need to have someone who knows perl go over your code with you.”

WHAT???

I tried again to explain that I wasn’t asking for them to fix my code, just give me the output.

He understood what I meant and suggested the same thing.

Tsk. That began the end of my dealings on that ISP.

The cost is a bit more here at webquarry, but I have access to the logs! And I have a shell account! And I get helpful customer service!!!

Yay!
