Chapters at Toshiba

By Rob Nugen

##17:21 Tuesday 17 February 2015 JST##

I love teaching at Toshiba because the topics are so sciencey and interesting! Today Tomomi met me in Kawasaki station and introduced me to some new people at Toshiba. I had gone in earlier, thinking I would have lessons with the attendants, but they were all like, “ummm not today!”

Good thing I called JB, who was like, “don’t worry. Wait for Tomomi and she’ll get you sorted.”

While there, I watched one of the demonstrations with liquid nitrogen.

Spoilers!:

They dip a flower then crush it, dip a rubber ball then drop (and shatter) it, dip an inflated balloon to watch it shrink (and reinflate), and dip an inflated clear plastic bag to watch it shrink as liquid appears on the inside.
