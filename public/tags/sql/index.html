29 Nov 1993, 00:00

SQL instead of Catherine Wheel

By Rob Nugen

(transcribed from Book Six 11:08pm JST Saturday 27 June 2009 (day 14339))

2:35 AM Tuesday 30 November 1993 Day 67

"Today" I did SQL stuff & looked for Cathy at SPB but I didn't find her.

SPB sponsored Catherine Wheel in concert in the Houston Room. i was security, but only 30 or so people came so I cruised to work on SQL.

I came back at the end of cleanup and helped take down the equipment.

I met Danny, the lighting guy who can throw a fucking fast overhand frisbee. It is a fresh maneuver. I can't draw it right now so I will just have to remember it.

He & I played disc out in the parking lot after the show & everything.

I flirted with Janice, Rosa, Jeanine,  Lisa on the way outside through OB on the way home.

Got Tanya's & my gymnastics routines printed and now we're all set.
