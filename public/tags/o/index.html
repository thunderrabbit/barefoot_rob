Best kids lessons so far

By Rob Nugen

##11:12 Wednesday 12 November 2014 JST##

And by “best” I mean, “well, I survived” though I’m mentally drained and have no idea how I can do it next week. I read the book “Go, Dog, Go” which they seemed to enjoy, but I didn’t see any other English books at the school.

I have no good way to start the lesson. I’ve just done a crappy Hello call and repeat thing but it’s kind of shite.

After the crappy opening, I’ve been doing “Walking Walking” which they definitely enjoy.

As I near the end of each song or section, I’m like “oh crap I have no idea what to do next!” And today there were parents watching! Eeeeeek!

The closing song, through crappy, has at least been consistent. The song I linked in previous entry is better and I hope I can remember it next time.
