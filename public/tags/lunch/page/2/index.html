lunch with M

By Rob Nugen

##11:51 Thursday 27 November 2014 JST##

Going to lunch with M, with whom I zip lined on Sunday.

##15:00:11

We ate at Wired Cafe on the east side of Shinagawa station, then walked two blocks east, then south along the river, then across the river on a blue bridge, and saw some bicycle rental place called “bike sharing” though we’d have to pay.

Continued south on the east side of the river to a large black metallic structure shaped like an O when viewed from the river, but like an n when looking parallel to the river.

We talked about travel and what we’d do if we had mo money. (Buy a house and not live in Japan.)
