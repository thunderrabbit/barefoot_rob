glass museum

By Rob Nugen

12:46 Saturday 19 August 2017 JST

Allow more time when going to the glass museum in Azumino. Or maybe just go early. We went around 10:45 and there was a queue for blowing glass until 1pm.

I did get to talk to a couple of guys down in the glass blowing room. Was soooo happy that they did not turn me away for being barefoot. I could easily imagine a rule, but apparently there was not. Anyway I had no problem with glass so it was fine. Anyway, I also had no time, so did not get to make a bottle or glass or anything. Not sure what I would have made. Probably a glass, since it would be most useful for me.
