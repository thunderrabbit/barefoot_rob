Hilarious! I just re-read her suggestion and realize she said “learn this like a song” I ready it as “learn something like a song“

For your homework, it would be great if you could review these
sentences so I can test you on them in the next lesson.
My homework style is:
1) Listen a lot, and remember the sentences like the lyrics of a song.
2) When you remembered the sounds in that way, you’ll get curious about the meaning of the sentences.
3) Look at the explanations I send you and study the meaning
4) Even if there are kanji that you can’t read, you’ll be able to read
the sentences because you remembered the pronunciation first

It’s a very simple, but totally effective method! If you remember
the sounds like song lyrics, your pronunciation will also get more
native-like, and you’ll be able to construct natural-sounding
sentences just by swapping out single words.
