how to get photos while building my speaker bio

By Rob Nugen

10:23 Saturday 27 January 2018 JST

I am taking Soness’s Build Your Speaker Bio course, learning techniques for how to get great photos for my bios. She brought in branding photographer Heidi Hapanowicz who is giving great tips on how to find a great photographer and what to expect from them. Expect to pay minimum of a few hundred bucks, but you should not have to micromanage nor dig through hundreds of photos. They should find the best 4 or 5 per outfit to show you.
