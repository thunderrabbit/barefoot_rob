Going to send Francine to Sydney

By Rob Nugen

19:05 Friday 11 March 2016 JST

Sydney decided she loved Francois and that he should be her rabbit, and I was like, “yeah nah” because I’ve had Francois longer than she’s been alive.

But, I said I’d be happy to send Francine to her.
