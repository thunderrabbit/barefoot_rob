walk twelve: brick fishtank

By Rob Nugen

06:32 Thursday 30 August 2018 JST

I heard some water trickling and noticed a fountain flowing into a basin made of bricks. There were a couple fish inside, effectively making it a brick fishtank.

The sky was overcast so not too hot.

Oh, today was the day I walked across part of the dirt lot and up into the back corner area. I didn’t think to take pics while on the inside, but I might go again.

Lins car hit by motorbike guys shoulder

By Rob Nugen

22:47 Wednesday 11 October 2017 JST

I am at a hospital with Lin after a little fender bender involving a guy’s shoulder hitting her mirror hard enough to cut her hand as the glass shattered into her open window.

Lin’s fine, and the guy’s apparently fine; goodness gracious I hope she starts being more careful.
