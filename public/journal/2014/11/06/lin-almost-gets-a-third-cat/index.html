Lin almost gets a third cat

By Rob Nugen

##23:15 Thursday 06 November 2014 JST##

Lin is trying to woo the little cat who has taken a liking to our balcony. She goes, “I’m afraid I might start to like him.”

I go, “too late!”

I won’t be surprised if we have three cats soon enough, but no more!
