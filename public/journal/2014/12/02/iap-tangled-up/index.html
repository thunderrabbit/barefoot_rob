IAP tangled up

By Rob Nugen

##12:28 Tuesday 02 December 2014 JST##

I’m trying to test the In-App Purchases for our new app, but for some reason all the descriptions are off. When I click this one it buys that one and when I buy that one it buys the other one. I thought I had all this shit worked out.

##13:02

Got it worked out. Apparently Apple returns the list of products in alphabetical order instead of by the order I list them in my code.
