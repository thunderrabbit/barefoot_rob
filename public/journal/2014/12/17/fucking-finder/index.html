fucking Finder

By Rob Nugen

##21:56 Wednesday 17 December 2014 JST##

More than once in recent memory, Finder has hung when trying to load images. It responds in every other way, but simply refuses to display the files in the Finder window where they should be displayed. Why?

It’s (fortunately) not the (SSD) drive dying; Emacs loads the list of files immediately, but Finder just hangs.

Even Terminal displays them instantly.

And it’s not like there’s a million files. In these cases, there are six (6) and two (2) files, respectively, less than 2MB each.
