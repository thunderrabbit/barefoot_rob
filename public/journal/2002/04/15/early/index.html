Early

By Rob Nugen

5:43am CDT Monday 15 April 2002

Slept mostly from 3pm to 9pm after the rally. Had a terrible headache most of the night past that. I ate some food; that didn't help. I had slept; that hadn't helped.

I think it might be cause my apartment was hot. Only like 80 degrees, but I felt better after going outside to do laundry.

After laundry, I visited work to catch up on email and CYA meeting stuff. (Committee on Youth Advisors)

Going back home to move laundry from washer to dryer and sleep some more.

5:16pm

I slept thoroughly this morning. 3 hours seemed like 20 minutes. 20 minutes seemed like 1 minute.

Today I've gotten some shit done, but it's taken a while to get momentum going, it seems. Got my taxes turned in as well.

7:20pm

Going to Wende's for dinner tonight.
