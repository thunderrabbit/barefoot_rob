Last night

By Rob Nugen

6:24 am Thursday 4 April 2002

Rolled over to University Schwinn last night to get my spoke fixed, but Jeff said he was too busy, and it would be okay to continue rolling around for a while with it broken. 'I've got two spokes broken on my bike,' he said, like it was a contest.

Painted three flags on a soap dish at Paint This!, which is just two shops down from the bike place, which is how I started going to that bike shop originally.

They look pretty good for the size brush I used... I felt rushed to get them done quickly; I wonder if I'll be happier with myself just getting it done, or doing a nicer job.

There's all sorts of questions in my head about this - I don't know how long each of them will be there (that's how I chose the flags - 3 countries are represented by the pees who live there), making the gift time-sensitive, in a way.

And it's a soap dish for goodness sakes. It will get soap scum on it, blocking the image anyway.

I'm not going to repaint it.

- - - -

Bicycle home, the drove to the Galleria to purchase Texas longhorn postcards (not the school, but the animal) to send to my new overseas friends, and to Gabriella, my work-related contact in Switzerland.
