1:57 pm Saturday 13 April 2002

Tom told us a brief version of the story of Lucy Decker (?) who started the first Gay Lesbian Straight Alliance in Massachusetts in 1992.

Amazing to think about a 14 year old starting this program and spreading it to other schools, and generating enough interest that the governor funded the programs with 500 thousand dollars.

Over years, she helped it grow to a huge rally thing of 7500 people in one day event.

what the world needs now is love sweet love. and more chocolate.

Laura wrote a random limerick about Lucy:

Lucy was one rockin' 'dyke'
She told prejudice 'take a hike!'
By starting GSAs
Things are  better for gays
Now she is one youth that we like.
