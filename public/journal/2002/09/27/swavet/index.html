SwaVet

By Rob Nugen

1:52pm CDT Friday 27 September 2002

Just called Swa'Vet, who I met during production of Dirty Third II, produced by Wreckshop Records. She gave me a bunch of phone numbers of different production companies and record labels and all sorts of shit. I'll probably call none of them, except maybe to find an address for Eminem, but hey I have them just in case.

At the end of our convo, she was all, "write me a card or some shit; don't fuck around and don't do it, aight?"

"Shit, you can even draw a picture on a piece of paper and send that to me I don't care just send me somethin'"

She is so funny!!
