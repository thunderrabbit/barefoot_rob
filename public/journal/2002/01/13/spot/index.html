spot

By Rob Nugen

6:23pm CST Monday 14 January 2002

On Sunday after HAC YAC meeting, where we finalized plans for Houston Rally, (which is this weekend!!!!!) I visited Paint This! and brought Spot so he would get some fresh air and new experiences instead of just being a lazy indoor cat:

He was great on the way there in the car. No crying or anything. When we arrived he was all claws in my shoulder at first, but then chilled and snooped around the shop. After about an hour or so, he vanished.

We found him sitting sneakily under a table on some boxes.

I'm glad he had a fun time.
