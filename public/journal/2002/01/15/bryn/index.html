Bryn!

By Rob Nugen

3:18pm CST Tuesday 15 January 2002

I just faxed a reservation for Bryn and I at the hostel in France! In London, we're going to get floor space at her friends apartment, which is awesomely cheaper. This is a guy from Egypt that she knows from OZ. So fuckin' awesome!

This weekend after the houston rally, Bryn and Elizabeth are going to stay for a night over at my apartment. I'm looking forward to that, but I figure we'll be too crazy tired to even talk much.

Still fun though!

I'm just so frickin' excited about this!
