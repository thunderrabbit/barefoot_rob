By Rob Nugen

pics

10:23pm CST Thursday 17 January 2002

Fun pictures recently, and I'm not going to give them each their own entry.

This is my name in the concrete. The 2002 doesn't show up particularly clearly, but I know it's there!

Last night as Dude, John, Gene and I were riding bikes around Whole Foods parking lot on Bellaire (Holcombe?), Gene fell off his bike in a super low speed low impact bizarre "how-the-hell-did-you-just-fall-over?" kind of way. He was kind enough to pose for the pic.

I took a pic of a bank on Kirby. My artsy-est picture yet!




Just tonight, Dude helped me pic up my old recliner, and Eric and Meredith’s futon. See, the recliner was hand-me-downed from my ex-ex-ex-ex girlfriend Christine’s parents. When Wende and I moved into our house, she didn’t think it matched the decor, so we gave it to Maggie. Now that I have my own apartment again, I asked Maggie if I could have it back.

Also, Eric and Meredith loaned a futon to Maggie's brother-in-law Kevin, and then he quit using it so they loaned it to me, with the understanding that I'll buy it when I have the money.

Dude helped me move them today, but we learned our lesson from when we moved the couch: get a bigger truck.
