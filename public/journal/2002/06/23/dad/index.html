dad

By Rob Nugen

6:21pm CDT Sunday 23 June 2002

My dad came over around noon today to hang out. We munched at Hobbit Cafe and then went to see Scooby Doo movie. Pretty funny. Especially funny is what we thought was most funny: the burp and fart contest by Shaggy and Scooby! Hilarious if you think burp and fart contests are hilarious.

Hung out a bit more at my apartment after grocery shopping and now he's gone back home. We'll likely have another Rob and Dad day around his birthday in September.
