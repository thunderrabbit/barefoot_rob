Ellen

By Rob Nugen

12:23am CDT Wednesday 5 June 2002

Turns out that Ellen used to be a DJ at KTRU, and she knows the super cute Sarah Pitre.

I haven't seen her since that Friday night (it's Tuesday now) I don't know if she has not fully moved in yet or what.
