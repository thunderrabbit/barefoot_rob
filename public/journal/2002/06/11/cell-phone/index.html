Cell phone

By Rob Nugen

11:51am CDT Tuesday 11 June 2002

It's so completely awesome to just have a cell phone. Just now I got a call from a Fed Ex guy who was standing at the front gate of my apartment complex. He has part of my new computer! (It's the 5 gig external portable drive) If I had a phone in my apartment, he would have just gotten no answer, and possibly would have taken the package back with him.

However, the front gate calls my cell phone when my apartment number is dialed; I asked him to drop it off with the leasing office. So excellent!
