Dream

By Rob Nugen

5:45am CDT Thursday 27 June 2002

(dream)

In a classroom situation, a student was outside with the intention of knocking down the flag pole in such a way that it would crash into the classroom.

The professor didn't seem too worried, saying something like "I wonder what it will be this time?"

I was all he's going to crash into the school; look out

But when I saw the plan unfold outside, it was less than menacing.

He was down the hill a ways riding on a park-sized train (that people can ride for fun around a park) and had tied a cable from the train to the flag pole. But he had tied the cable in a direct line to the flagpole, so that when the train moved forward, the cable would be routed around a tree, severely limiting the destructive force of the train.

The train rolled along the track and the student noticed the cable was now bent around the tree, so he cut the cable and went to run up around the tree and back to the train, but then the somehow ran over the cable so he couldn't attach it... such a mess was made of his plan.

It turns out the cable was a high density fiber optic data cable, so the student was in a ton of trouble for cutting it and letting a train run over it. I was put in the position of yelling at the student, but noisy people were all around me being noisy and the student was a 6 or so year old girl who was quite uncomforable with the whole situation. I was glad to wake up.
