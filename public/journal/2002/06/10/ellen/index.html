Ellen

By Rob Nugen

11:09 pm Monday 10 June 2002

Ellen has begun to move in. As I was chillin' in my underwear on the living room floor, I heard someone walking by and down the stairs. I looked up and she waved at me.

Ellen? I wondered. It had to be her. Woohoo!

I tried to think of something smart to say as I put on my shorts to go outside and presumably help her move stuff in. I looked over the balcony as she was using the broken cinder blocks to prop open the gate. She succeeded and I walked down the steps and into the parking lot before I said HI.

She jumped a bit and I felt bad a bit. Doh. I shoulda said something sooner.

I helped her bring up a single load and then she left for Walgreens to get toiletries.

I look forward to falling out of love with her so we can just be friends and I can quit being so silly.
