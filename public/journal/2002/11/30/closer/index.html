closer

By Rob Nugen

3:44pm PST Saturday 30 November 2002

As I drove from Kinko's to Sally's NIN "closer" was on 94.9, the radio station I found in San Diego. When I called her number, I could hear her actual phone ringing; her apartment is visible from the front of the building. Either that, or in an incredible irony, someone else's phone rang exactly the same times as the speaker's phone rang on the keypad on multiple times that I dialed. I'm getting closer to saying HI to Sally...

4:36pm

Made it! They were out riding bikes for an hour and had just arrived back when I tried calling again. We're going swimming now! I'm hoping to get some just Rob-n-Sally time esp. so I can inquire about last night's apparent wackiness.
