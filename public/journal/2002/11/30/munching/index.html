munching

By Rob Nugen

11:21pm PST Saturday 30 November 2002

Munched dinner at Thaifoom (?), a Thai restaurant on (get this:) Fashion Island.

Fashion Island.

Fashion Island is not an island. It's just a glorified shopping center have I already said this? Anyway, whatever; who cares.

Dinner was lovely and included sake, which was poured to overflowing, symbolizing money is no concern.

I had a meal called "Evil Jungle Princess." Basically chicken and peanut sauce and green beans and rice.
