8 Mile

By Rob Nugen

11:55pm CST Sunday 17 November 2002

Just got back from seeing 8 Mile with Josephine. Jack and Janette had come too, but the movie times didn't work out well, so they went back home. I am thankful to Janette for her willingness to come pick us up after our movie.

I had put the tickets on my credit card, and when we refunded their two tickets, the guy gave me cash back. So that's a sneaky way to get cash on my credit card without knowing the pin number.

The movie was great. I was hyped during the battle scenes at the end. Some cat in the theater said the movie was dumb for ending the way it did. He just don't understand.

- - - -

In the theater, there were two young children, like 2 or 3 years old.

Also, the guy in front of us answered a phone call and made a phone call during the movie. I kicked his seat forward and then immediately felt sorry for being equally disrespectful as he.
