zzzz rave

By Rob Nugen

19 March 2002

I went to a rave last night, where a girl smiled big at me while we were dancing and then subsequently approached me, put her arms on my shoulders and said, "I want to cuddle" and then something, and I was all, "I'll dance with you, but that's it." She asked if I was drunk (not like, Oh my gosh you don't want to cuddle; you must be drunk) and then asked if I knew anyone that could help her get something. (As I type this, "ABC" by The Jackson Five is being fed through the speakers at this internet cafe. Same one I was at yesterday, but this time I purchased a 24 hour pass for £4. Now it's "(I can't get no) satisfaction" by whoever sings that.) I was all, "um, no, but good luck." Then she's all, "are you from around here?" I told her no and it turns out she's from Chicago. She backed off and then when she left she gave me a cheek to cheek kiss and "nice to meet you." "You too." mwah!
