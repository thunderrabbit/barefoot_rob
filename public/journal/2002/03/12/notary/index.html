Notary

By Rob Nugen

10:32am CST Tuesday 12 March 2002

I just got a thing notarized so Wende can sign for me at our house closing while I'm galavanting around London and Paris with Bryn! How ironic that I've been putting off the notarization because of the inconvenience and I find out that Jerri across the hall at work is a notary. She didn't even charge me, so I'll send her a postcard.

This is even better than Mia in Pulp Fiction at Jack Rabbit Slims when she goes to the restroom and comes back to find her food waiting. I'm going to Europe, come back and my house will be sold!

12:31pm same day

I have just returned from lunch with Wende; I gave her the notarized form, helped her turn in the checks for investment club; exchanged $137-ish for 90 British pounds. Saw Mark Leonard from ultimate there at the Galleria. Actually he saw me first. Saw Amy as well. Actually she saw me first.

Saw the Build-A-Bear store, where one can pick the fur, stuffing, clothes, accessories for a new teddy bear or rabbit or others I'm sure. Wow. It was relatively packed with 5 - 7 year old girls and moms.
