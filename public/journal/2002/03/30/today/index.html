30 Mar 2002, 00:00

today

By Rob Nugen

1:42pm Saturday 30 March 2002

After Maggie-sitting, I drove to KTRU where Abi was finishing up the Children's Show, which she does every Saturday from noon to 1pm.

Before I arrived, she played my request "Little Noah Brill" by Momus, off his 2CD set Stars Forever, which I reviewed in 1999 (April I think). Thanks, Abi for playing it!

I went to the station to give her the Salvador Dali print that I received in London after visiting the exhibit of his work.

Wonderfully, Jordan arrived just before I intended to leave, which was great news because I gifted to him the KTRU sticker that visited London and Paris and is in many photographs that I took during that trip. I gave him the sticker because he volunteered to cover my shift tonight while I'm at the play with Kristen and Jessica (sisters) to see Katharine and Catherine (homonyms).

Now I'm at Dude's house with plans to begin composing my journal entries and pictures from the trip. But his computer with all the pictures on it locked up when I opened Photoshop. I just checked. It's still locked. 3 finger salute doesn't work; gotta hit reset, it seems.
