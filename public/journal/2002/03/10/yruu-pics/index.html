yruu pics

By Rob Nugen

afternoon CST Sunday 10 March 2002

YRUU was fun and chillin' today. I took pics of people there, except for Smom, who had to leave early.

Hethre is from our local YRUU; she is excellent in photojournalism; wants to be a concert promoter for some cool band or artist. She's editor of This Is Bad, and one more new zine the nname of which I forgot. A one word thing like Smelg.

The next three are from Beaumont; they may start coming once per month because they like us.

Laura has been amazingly active in our district and continental YRUU. She's been on YAC and has been our YCR, has served on Youth Council, and maybe Steering Committee! Laura = wow.

Jack is amazing in his life history. He always wins the Ten Fingers game, where people say, "I have never ....." and fill in the blank with something they have never done. Whoever has done that has to lower a finger. Jack always in the last with fingers up.

Natalie is a friend of Jack and Laura's; I don't know much about her. Okay; I know nothing about her. But she was cool and fit right in with us.

Jeff is another advisor for the local YRUU. He has been to a few rallies and always wears that funny hat. He fits right in at the Rennaisance Festival!
