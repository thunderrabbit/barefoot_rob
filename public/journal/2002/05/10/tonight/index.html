10 May 2002, 00:00

tonight

By Rob Nugen

9:00 pm Friday 10 May 2002

I would have ridden my unicycle home, but the crankshaft decided to not be attached to the wheel. it's still inside the wheel, but it turns and the wheel doesn't.

I just visited Ridg, a dad or something, step dad i think of John, friend of Dude's. Helped Ridg with computer stuff. Win 98. pretty simple. He seems like a nice guy and all; I may just pay him a visit for fun and dialogue.

Going to meet Marta at TGI Friday's out on I-10 and the beltway. She's the Marta that I met at Plano Rally. Her dad's friend is the recruiter for English teachers to Japan.

She brought information for me on that. Nice.

11:40 pm

Kristen called during the evening and it turns out that she was in Katy, so she came bby to say HI for a bit. She and Marta exchanged a few stage performance stories.

Kristen just left and I just found out that Marta and I have the same birthday five years apart. She's 9908 days old today and will be 10000 on 10 August 2002!

Also learned that our waiter Quincy is a hip hop artist.

12:34 am Saturday 11 May 2002

Super thanks to Marta for a lovely meal.

Had a great time talking to Quincy, whose stage name is Fateye (two words maybe). He is an up and coming hip hop artists, just trying to make a few benjamins in his life.

Going to my dad's now to drive to Tyler tomorrow.

2:22 am Saturday

I'm at my dad's house now. The kitchen looks significantly different. I'm sad that they painted over the wood paneliing inside the kitchen. When we bought the house 15+ years ago, the wood paneling was a big reason why my dad liked it.

I remember being proud of him defending his preference against our loud-mouth neighbor who said, "there's too much wood"

Even so, great to joke around with Fred - all silly stories and funniness.

Oh, and I get to see dad's sister Becky and their mom Cordie, and Becky's granddaughter Sarah (sp) who looks just like her mom Barbie. So amazing.

2:48 am

Wow. I just read through the wedding ceremony plan, and note that I am the Best Man for my dad. I guess that's why he asked me to bring a suit.
