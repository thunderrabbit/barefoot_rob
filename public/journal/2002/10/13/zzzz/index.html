12:18am EDT Monday 14 October 2002

I want to eat. But actually I just feel sad and want to eat to block that emotion. I'm sad about not going to San Antonio Rally. I'm going to miss you guys like crazy.

I love you guys so much. You're so smart and capable and beautiful. So funny and daring and awesome. Thank you for the opportunity to help you grow.
