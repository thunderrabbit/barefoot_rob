06 Oct 2002, 00:00

barbie

By Rob Nugen

6:06pm EDT Sunday 6 October 2002

Barbie is so beautiful. Too bad she smokes and is my cousin, but I just really think she's hot.

She's an awesome mom for Sarah and Shane, and is super success oriented in her career. She's working on buying a magazine (and I ain't talking about buying 1 issue of a magazine) and has done lots of other stuff, but I don't know what yet until we get a real chance to talk!

6:11pm

At this moment, Keely and Sarah and Bevin have come over to visit, further interrupting Barbie, but they are about to split and go fishing; they were just checking in with Barbie.

Sarah is in a super great mood; she loves playing with Keely.

Interesting that Barbie likes to have Sarah dressed nicely even when she (Sarah) is about to go fishing.

8:04pm

Barbie and I have finally gotten to talk. We walked around her neighborhood; there is a circular road that basically wraps around most of the houses and holes in the golf course. Showed me the riverfront lot she purchased, the last buildable lot in Barboursville which she apparently got for a good price.

Exchanged relationship stories of both of our misadventures in sex/romance. whoopie.

Told her a bit about my love of youth advising, and my final two planned rallies before I go. How Bryn told me if I let YRUU keep me from exploring the world, then that's what I'll be teaching the youth: let something great hold me back from the world.

We waved to three people walking by and I noticed it was Jason's mom and two of her kids. "HI Jason's mom!" and me-an-Barbie's conversation was interrupted while they talked neighbor stuff and work stuff. They have both worked in radio before. (behind the scenes)

Barbie and I will probably chat again soon.

1:06am

Chatted with Barbie, shared a bit more about relationship stuff and my love of beautiful Sally. Barbie and I seem to share a tendency to fall in love with people pretty quickly (and usually fall out of love pretty quickly too.)
