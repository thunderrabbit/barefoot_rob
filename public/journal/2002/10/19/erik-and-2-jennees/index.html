Erik and 2 jennees

By Rob Nugen

4:47pm CDT Saturday 19 October 2002

I'm in Springfield and Amelia and Erik's house. Erik was unimpressed with my Mac until he really started looking at it. I told him I wouldn't tell anyone, so um, nevermind.

He got a phone call and hung up saying, "soon there will be two Jenni's here."

"You can't go wrong with two Jenny's," I agreed.

At this very moment there are 2 Jenni's sitting on Erik and tickling him, as he tries in vain to squirm away. Wow, how can one guy endure such torture?

hahahaha

Erik is a big guy. Each Jenny is about half his mass. What a funny sight!

5:35pm

Now, Jenny wants Erik to do errands with her and Jenni wants Erik to magically have already downloaded some anime for her. Jenny was pouting gloriously while Jenni was griping that he wasn't ready to go yet.

"It's not fair for both of you to be mad at me at the same time!"
