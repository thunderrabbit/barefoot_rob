Reading to Janette

By Rob Nugen

10:44pm CDT Tuesday 22 October 2002

Janette made bread tonight and while it cooked, I read to her most of the first 6 chapters of Stranger in a strange land, which I have never previously read. According to the preface, this version is from the original transcript, not the one that was originally published in 1960-something.

Pretty good story.

I appreciate that it was written over a long period of time. That helps me have hope for The Past is Now.
