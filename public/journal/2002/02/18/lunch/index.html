Lunch

By Rob Nugen

2:52pm CST Monday 18 February 2002

Just got back from lunch. Kristen broke me out of jail (*) for a bit so we could get a snack at Amy's Ice Cream. She even fronted me 75 cents for my Tiny sized snack.

We played ten fingers and she won pretty handily (no pun intended) with four fingers left.

First and last hug before she goes back to DC tomorrow.

(*) jail = work
