8 Mile

By Rob Nugen

8:15pm CST Monday 30 December 2002

Colleen, Janette and I went to see 8 Mile today. It was Colleen's first viewing, Janette's second, my third. I loved it loved it loved it as much as the first time, or maybe more.

Colleen wasn't as blown away as I appeared to be, as I busted out of the theater, "that movie crushes all other movies!" and decided once and for all I'm going to write to Eminem and thank him for all his creativity.
