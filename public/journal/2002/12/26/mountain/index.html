Mountain

By Rob Nugen

6:01pm PST Thursday 26 December 2002

Just climbed (drove up) a mountain about 90 miles north of LA. I wonder how high it is. Getting gas now.

(actually I just need to pee and am getting gas to be nice.)

6:07pm

Awww man. No restrooms working at that gas station, so I came across the street and *these* are out too! "What's up with the restrooms?" "Water main is out; it broke yesterday from freezing."

D'oh!

And this cat wants 79 cents for Reese's Pieces, too. Mannnnn.

4144 feet.

6:42pm

4686 miles traveled. now I'm in the LA traffic jam. and it's 36 miles to LA according to the sign.

7:06pm

Fuck that noise. I turned around to take the exit that some signs encouraged due to the traffic. I'm on 126 now and will basically take the 1 to Newport Beach.

4695 miles traveled.

7:32pm

Ahhhhhh.

Working restroom in the Chevron in Fillmore, California, corner of 126 and 23. *And* Reese's Pieces for 70 cents each, *including tax*.

Dat's what I'm talkin' about.

4714 miles traveled.

8:34pm

Super twisty mc twisting up around this big mountainy area with practically no reflectors (definitely no street lights) and beautiful houses overlooking beautiful overlooks. Gotta be a movie star neighborhood here.

Ohhh and one is for sale. Can't see the house, but I see the sign: 310-589-2461 Barbara Horton selling a property on 23 south.

Amazing houses on 23. 4749 miles traveled. Curves everywhere very little traffic. Beautiful starry sky.

8:40pm

Another property. This one at address 4100 south 23. 310-990-2079 Matt Ogden.

Buy one of these properties and tell the realator to give me a finder's fee, and with the fee I'll throw you a house warming party!
