26 Dec 2002, 00:00

i-10

By Rob Nugen

9:20pm PST Thursday 26 December 2002

Muwahahahahahaaaa!!!

I have now seen where I-10 dumps into the ocean. I had previously only been on I-10 as far as Palm Springs. (Not driving! but when I flew to Con Con 1998, I also visited Jennifer in Palm Springs, and part of the adventure included me driving on I-10)

They have an amusement park to celebrate I-10's arrival at the Pacific! Right here in Santa Monica. Whoohooo!!!

9:49pm

4789 miles traveled.

Got lost in Santa Monica, but too tired to totally enjoy it.

10:08pm

Trying to get out of Santa Monica, following the 1 and suddenly... BANG! without warning I end up at LAX! Los Angeles International Airport??? Woah!

I had no idea it was so close to Santa Monica. I got a bit lost in the airport area, but now after a glance at the map I think I'm set. 105 to 405 and I'll be back on a familiar freeway.

10:54pm

After 70 - 80 mph LA driving on crowded 405 (I could get used to this style of driving), I'm at Sally's aptartment complex. If she's not home, I'll go to Kinko's and upload all these entries.

4842 miles since Dallas.

Approximately 800 miles today. (797)
