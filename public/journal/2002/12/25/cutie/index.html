cutie

By Rob Nugen

7:32pm PST Wednesday 25 December 2002

Oh my gosh what a cutie at this gas station. Red hair and braces. Oh so cute!

8:30pm

My god. It's been an hour and I'm still fixating on that cute woman. I should have said something, anything to her. Well I did, but didn't give her my card. I coulda asked her how to get to the freeway or said I'm on a roadtrip, or fucking anything. Yargh. Why was I so scared to do that?

9:53pm

Okay. I seem to have kinda sorta thought of some reasons to get over it. The best two are (!) trust the universe. (@) there's lots of cute people that go by all the time in cars, but I don't know it cause I can't see them.

Whatever.

I've now traveled 4045 miles since Dallas. I'm at a Vista on 299, some miles east of 101 in California (about 50 or so miles south of Redwood Forest I'm guessing). Time for sleeeep.

Haha 333 miles traveled today.
