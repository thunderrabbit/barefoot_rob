Bicycle shopping

By Rob Nugen

2:35pm PST Thursday 12 December 2002

Been riding around Huntington Beach today, looking to purchase new gloves, and new liner for my skate helmet. I don't wanna buy a whole new helmet cause I like the stickers on this helmet:

  • KTRU (black rightside up, yellow upside down)
  • Fly High Little Bunny ("Bunny" in the front and "Fly High" plus the bat head in the back.)
  • Alien Workshop
  • NIN
  • ROCES Nuclear Power Under Your Feet
  • Senate

Stopped in a few different shops and got new gloves from Seaside Schwinn, and new helmet liner from Play It Again Sports. I like Play It Again Sports owner, Sean (sp), who was quite customer service conscious even while busy as fuck. I commented on this, and he said he took over the store some months ago, and profit has been going up ever since.

"Why?"

"Better personality, for one thing; taking care of the customers. Plus not stealing from the store. Little things like that...."

"hahah wow."

"Yeah, I've basically fired everyone who was working here - fired six people in the last month."

"Rebuild your staff from the bottom up."

He doesn't have a website, but they're at the corner of Harbor and Victoria in Costa Mesa. 949-548-7852.

My bike is ready. Tightened the back brake.
