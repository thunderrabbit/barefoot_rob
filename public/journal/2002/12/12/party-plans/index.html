party plans

By Rob Nugen

9:46pm PST Thursday 12 December 2002

Woah.

I'm going to miss the party at RSA Inline, but Sally and I are going to fly to San Francisco for a double-birthday-plus-homecoming party tomorrow night! (Jennifer and someone's birthdays are today or tomorrow, plus Christine is returning from Costa Rica)

I've never done anything quite so... wild in my life.

$118 for round trip tickets, one day in advance.

Oh my god... I just realized that we'll be coming back on Sunday, which is the day I planned to drive up to San Francisco. How nutty is life???

We'll go sailing on Sunday to see the seals.
