Email works

By Rob Nugen

9:56am PST Friday 6 December 2002

YES!!!

Thanks to Marcel's suggestion, buried in a tome of wit, I figured out how to change the port that OS X mail app uses to send mail because Kinko's has blocked port 25.

I also therefore had to see how to tell robnugen.com to use another port to listen to email.

Searched for DaemonPortOptions in the sendmail.cf and found that an alternate port had already been set up!! Whoooo!

Now why didn't tech support at webquarry tell me about the alternate port when I told them I was having problems???

Ah well; I don't care; I'm happy to be back in emailland again!
