23 Jul 2002, 00:00

Ardmore

By Rob Nugen

1:00pm CDT Tuesday 23 July 2002

During lunch I shunned the advisor table to go meet some new youth. I just met Marissa and Sarah. Marissa apparently dances crazily like me, according to Ellen. I asked Sarah if this was like what she expected. "Yes." ... "Laid back confusion." Nice.

5:53pm

Now we've basically finished the Ardmore cleanup. The press we create has been increasing each year. Last year they had an article in the Ardmore Press, and this year we were featured on KVII (I think (K something II for sure)) Joan and Sean were both interviewed. We loaded tons of shit into a big ass dumpster, and then Matt, Aaron, Erin and I were drumming freestyle on the desks and such in the pile.

Oh, I freestyle rapped a bit, and it wasn't terrible. That was a first for me. It was mostly bad, but some good. :-)

Tired sweaty tired sweaty.

6:13pm

On the bus now, going to Applebees as one hugemongous sweaty mass of people filling three school buses.
