By Rob Nugen

1:42am CDT Monday 15 July 2002

Fred and I are at House of Pies right now. He's coding perl.

Here's one of the regular expressions he used for the new journal code:.

$ls1r =~ m!^\./(\d\d\d\d/[^:]*?):\s*$(.*?\n\n)!smg

3:02am

At home now; I have to be up at 6:30. Well, 7, but I'll be more relaxed if I get up at 6:30.

Oh, on the way home, we encountered a water sprinkler spraying a large volume of water in an arc into the street. Traffic on Kirby was minimal, so we stopped underneath it, rolled back and forth a bit like it was a car wash.

Water hitting the roof sounded just like a mechanical car wash and we laughed and hollered like little kids. Fun stuff.
