afternoon

By Rob Nugen

3:50pm CDT Wednesday 3 July 2002

Oh my gosh. I've been signing things as the 2nd of July. Hmm. Oh well.

This morning I helped Wende get some boxes to her storage unit and get my desk and end table out of her storage unit. Then used her home phone to call AAA and sign up for their roadside assistance plan in preparation for my adventure.

Wandered around slightly aimlessly; this will take some getting used to. I visited First Unitarian Church on Fannin, looking to say HI to Gail the minister, but she wasn't there. I was also hoping to find some free food there, but none was readily available.

Went to Suzanne's work, but decided not to bug her, so I went to the Clayton Library across the street. Looked for my mom's parents in the 1930 census, but couldn't find them. I assume they were in Louisiana, and I think I used the soundex correctly, but didn't find them.

Left the library and saw a tour bus going to visit Suzanne's work, and lo, there was Suzanne outside her work, greeting them. So I merged in with the group and said HI to her while she greeted the rest of the group. She invited me in so I stayed for a bit and had a tomato from someone's garden.

Now I'm at Wende's work, where I'll go to lunch with her.

I got an HIV test after saying HI to Suzanne. I found that if I get blood work done, I can get a free juice or cookie. Got a pint of orange juice. It's free except that I paid $40 for the test.

Maybe I'll give plasma. Need I have to have a legal residence to do that? Hmmm.
