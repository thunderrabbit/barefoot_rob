Yoga

By Rob Nugen

10:33pm CDT Wednesday 17 July 2002

Yoga was fun, or at least different because I was at an actual class that Catherine was teaching as a substitute teacher for her instructor who normally teaches the class at The Fitness Exchange.

That was after Dad had come by and picked up the futon and the nightstand I made in 9th grade. Wende was there at the same time to consult on clothes packing for SWUUSI and my trek.

- - - -

After hanging out with Katy and Fred at Amy's Ice Cream and my apartment, where Fred essentially finalized the core components of my new journal indexing mechanism, I visited Catherine and dropped off the three drawer file cabinet to her apartment.

She gave me a sample of the protein drink bottles she gave me yesterday or so, and introduced me to stevia, a super sweetening herb. Wow; it's pretty sweet!

I gave her feedback on the yoga class, which she seemed to appreciate.

We're going to dinner tomorrow; she's taking me to Bombay Palace as a going away present.
