By Rob Nugen

5:48pm CDT Friday 12 July 2002

The most unique thing I've done today was play tennis with Amy in the street in front of her house. I had been at Container Store getting some boxes, and the wondered what time her tennis match is tonight. I didn't want to use a phone call, so I just drove over to her house. She asked if I wanted to bonk some tennis balls around and we did.

7:15pm

Now I'm with Steve, Amy's husband, watching her play tennis at some tennis place in southwest Houston. I think she is beating this woman, but I'm not totally sure. Okay, I'm pretty sure Amy is winning.

There's not a lot of people watching relative to the number of players, but I guess that is a result of what level is this competition. It's one step down from the state level.

My cell phone was turned to silent, but when Wende called, I noticed and pulled it out of my pocket and discovered consciously that it was ringing. cool.

7:27pm

Amy won the first thingie.

8:38pm

Amy didn't win the second and third thingies; the other Amy won instead.

Team is showing lots of love.
