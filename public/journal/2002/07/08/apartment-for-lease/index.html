Apartment for lease

By Rob Nugen

10:57am CDT Monday 8 July 2002

I'm exploring different ways to advertise the availability of my apartment. I've advertised via my website, telling my friends, Houston Press, Houston Chronicle, and just today I made some two sided business cards to hand out to people. These are on light blue paper instead of yellow like my Adventure business cards.

They'll be ready by 3pm today.

I went to Whataburger to check in with the manager who does the hiring. He interrupted me soon after I said, "I turned in an application yesterday..."

At first I thought he was just going to say, "Tuesday is my hiring day," which is what the employees had said yesterday. But he said something like, "I'm not even talking to anyone now; I'm going on vacation tomorrow, so come back in two weeks."

I love the irony. In two weeks, I intend to be out of here.

- - - -

In another ironic twist, I got a call from Nate, who was one of the first people I called right after I lost my job. He apologized for not having called back sooner. I told him my availability had changed since no one called me back... He'll keep my resume on file in case he gets a contract that can be finished before I leave.
