Spot

By Rob Nugen

2:56pm CDT Friday 19 July 2002

I feel really really really really really really sad.

I carried all of Spot's stuff down to the car - food, litterbox, scoop, treats, catnip.. then came back up for Spot and found him lounged on the floor.. I just started to bawl. So sad to give Spot away. All these tears pouring up my face with my head buried on the carpet.

Spot was all, "what the hell is all that noise?" and climbed up to the windowsill to lounge some more.

Super thanks to Jennifer for taking him on a moment's notice. She was going to take him, but didn't know that she was going to take him today. I called her about 30 minutes ahead of time. She gracefully said no problem; he'll just sleep over at Paint This! tonight, since she's not going home after work.

I wonder if he'll become a Paint This! cat. That would be pretty cool.
