Lake Games now!

5:32pm Monday 22 July 2002

Lake Games were fun. Noah and I nearly won the chicken fight against Mattboy and Mikey. But we lost.

There are people in the freezer right now.

Chicken fights were fun, though Abbey doesn't quite understand the fun. Noah and I assured her they were quite safe and tame and such. Just then Asha hurt her back a bit, bending backwards.

Okay, they're relatively safe.

A chauffered Asha on her rickshaw-ish floatie, and she pulled me a bit, but mostly not able to touch the bottom of the lake.

We almost instantly lost the two huge beach balls, but a boat recovered them for us. Then we lost one of them again about an hour later.

Just before the end of the lake adventure, I told Sean and Abbey "we have to get out of the lake soon, so I'm going up now to get first dibs on shower." Just then, Wende and Susie were walking down the dock to becon people out.

In shower we sang "The ants go marching" and "Paul Revere" and started "(You gotta) Fight For Your Right (to Party)", but didn't get too far.
