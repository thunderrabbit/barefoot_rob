By Rob Nugen

8:27pm CDT Sunday 21 July 2002

I got the opportunity to listen to Erin share about stuff in her life. The culmination of this event was her eating for the first time in two days.

I missed the advisor meeting, and therefore haven't signed up for rover duty. Eeep.

Now I'm down by the lake but, again, there's only one advisor (me).

8:31pm

Ah, Steve is here. [splash splash splash splash]

9:29pm

Cake: on the table, on the stereo, on Tanner, Bryn, Chris.

Tonight is the first night dance. Lots of peeps chilling and dancing and such.

Debbie hollering, "No! Just let one song play all the way through! I like that song. leave it right there. what is the problem???"

11:03pm

Time for worship soon. We have successfully had the first night's check in at our touch groups. Our group got to meet Hunter from Houston, new to our group.

We'll probably have a Silent Football game after worship. I'm not sure though.

11:28pm

David and I just figured out another way to play the psychic number game. We can get numbers up to 63, but we're sticking with 50 to make our technique less obvious. We also amazed Chez by communicating "Penelope Cruz" and "Jimmy Fallon" Nice.

Worship soon maybe.

12:17am

I stood between Bryn and Wende at worship. Good thing we were all holding hands in the circle cause I was falling asleep, using them to keep my balance while standing (and sleeping).

Bryn wore no sleeves, had a bit of trouble with mosquitoes.

Nice first night worship. Very appropriate.

1:05am

Calder is leading a Silent Football game. I'm thrilled about that; she is even showing new people how to play. I hope the game goes well. I won't be there for I have set up my bed on the road by the lake.

2:28am

Bryn and Chris came down to sleep as well.
