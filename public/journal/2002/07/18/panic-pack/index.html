Panic Pack

By Rob Nugen

11:02am CDT Thursday 18 July 2002

I was up until 5am last night working on my website, not working on moving. Time to pack. Panic Pack. :-)

8:36pm

At around 4:30pm Wende helped me pack my clothes for SWUUSI and my trek. Super duper special thanks to her for that.

At Bombay Palace with Catherine. Funniest thing was when the train went by and I was all, "is it going this direction?" and indicated with my thumb, and she nodded yes, then goes, "no," and starts moving her eyes as if watching it go by the other direction. Funny!

To Catherine's house so I could read a bit from her Culture Shock India book. Then big hug and I'll miss you's.

12:32am

Fred and I arrived at Kinko's and Swa'vet was here with Greg. As they were leaving Swa'vet was all, "Rob. Call me tomorrow."

"I'm leaving tomorrow."

"Then call me in the morning before you leave."

"What's your number?"

As Swa'vet was getting her card out of her purse, Junie and Greg were both talking, actually all three of them were, and she gave me her card in a multi-tasking type way, then as she left says to me, "Rob, stop fucking around and call me tomorrow."

Fred and I laughed for several minutes at that.
