14 Jul 2017, 07:53

Going to Renee's in Gunma today

By Rob Nugen

07:53 Friday 14 July 2017 JST

Leaving in 2 hours.

18:51 Friday 14 July 2017 JST

Such a nice area! We went to a long cave thing that was apparently dug without modern equipment. It is 400 meters long, and was apparently supposed to go 400 meters more, but life and heavy rock got in the way. Really nice quiet energy there.

Visited a 41.8 meter tall statue which is 10 meters taller than Corcovado in Rio. So interesting because my memory of Corcovado is that it was taller. Like way taller. Is it an illusion? Faulty memory?

We went to the daruma shrine after going to a daruma shop where they had hundreds of different daruma in a wide variety of sizes and colors, including some branded with sports teams logos.

Francois has been featured in many many photos.

Finally sent book back to Margaret

By Rob Nugen

16:32 Tuesday 11 July 2017 JST

I finally got Margaret’s book on its way back to her. She lent it to me years ago.. like 8 years and said she would like to have it back sometime.. and then our group ended and she left the country.
