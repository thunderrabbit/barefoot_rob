By Rob Nugen

18:34 Tuesday 06 October 2015 JST

The best new puzzle was a wooden chain like a bicycle chain except some of its links were fixed, as if they were rusty and wouldn’t rotate, except they were actually uncut pieces of wood. Apparently, it’s possible to arrange the chain on a tree shaped board so that all the links exactly cover the tree, including one at the bottom for the trunk.
