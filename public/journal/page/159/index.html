By Rob Nugen

13:21 Saturday 16 January 2016 JST

I’ve decided that I like all electric for practical reasons: harder to start a fire by forgetting chicken in the broiler; I also like it for optimistic reasons: I like to think in the next N years while we live there, zero-point energy technology will be open-sourced so we can dramatically reduce our energy costs.

As I’m writing this, Lin’s writing “shit stuff” on some kind of registration form for the real estate company who will take us to see the house. The family is still in the house, so it will be interesting to see their gear, but a bit like “hi sorry we’re in your house!”

Sunday 13:41

Oh my gosh we loved it and loved them! After seeing the house and the surrounding area, we told the real estate agent YES, and we’ll see if the bank agrees.

The house is fucking fantastic and in a great location. Plus great

I'm seriously thankful for the place
