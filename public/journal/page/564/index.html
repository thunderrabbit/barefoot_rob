Toilet

By Rob Nugen

5:15am 15 February 2001

The bathroom in this house does not have a lock on the door. And this is the bathroom off the main hallway of the house. I don't know if that is common in OZ, or if it's just a quirk of this house.

The toilet is even more interesting. There is no "handle" to flush the toilet. On the top of the tank, there are 2 buttons. One smaller than the other. Each of them flushes the toilet when pressed. The smaller button uses less water than the big one, and they each use less water than a common US toilet. The tank is smaller than a US toilet tank, so even using both of them together uses less water than a US toilet.
