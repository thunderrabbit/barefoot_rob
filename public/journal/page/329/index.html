Work

By Rob Nugen

11:09pm JST Thursday 18 September 2003

Okay, very quickly, just in case I ever care:

Had my 6 month review's counseling today. First Steve was all, "I'll just be watching, not ganging up on you," but he spoke as much or more than J-lo (Jon Lo) did, and I called him on it. "I'm distracted because I feel like I'm being tagged teamed. Okay, what was the question?"

The second 40 minutes included no Steve so that was a relief. I don't think I'll ever learn (care to learn) the Nova way of doing things and I told J-Lo straight up that I'm not going to re-sign a contract with Nova. Steve later said, "well you can't just give up," and I'm thinking, "really?" But we'll see what I judge is in my own best interest from moment to moment.

Neil Hasagawa-Yates (sp) is the AAM (Assistant Area Manager?), Steve's Boss, and he offices at Kawasaki now. Today was the first time I've talked to him, and he seemed more... down to earth than cramming Nova down my ears like some people seem to do. He had some useless rhetoric, but somehow I was left thinking I could possibly talk to this cat, even though he seems to be so far removed from what we do in the trenches. I know he's been through it all, but..yeah whatever. Okay, that I didn't *hate* N H-Y is what I wanted to write.

I haven't finished the entry above and I probably never will; more important to work on my speech. I have basically memorized the first two lines.

My name is Rob Nugen.

私はRob Nugenです。　　　



I was born in the US in 1970 and grew up with my brother and parents in Texas.
私は　１９７０ねんに　アメリカで　うまれて　テキサスで　りょうしんと　きょうだいと　いっしょに　すんでいました。



Texas is situated in the southern central United States,
bordering Mexico and the Gulf of Mexico on the southwest and southeast.
テキサスは　アメリカの　みなみちゅうおうにあり、メキシコと　こっきょうをせっし　なんせいぶと　なんとうぶの　メキシコわんに　めんしています。



Four other states border Texas on the west, north and east sides.
また、テキサスの　にしと　きたと　ひがしがわは　４つのしゅうと　めんしています。



Texas is almost twice as big as Japan, with some big cities near the south and east borders,
and a few big cities scattered within vast plains and plateaus in west and north Texas.
テキサスのおおきさは　にほんの　やく２ばいで、みなみと　ひがしの　しゅうざかいに　おおきなとしが　いくつかあります。　
また、２，３の　だいとしが  Texas にしときたの　こうだいな　へいげんや　こうげんに　てんてんとあります。



Texas is famous for cowboys, made popular by Hollywood movies.
テキサスは　ハリウッド　えいがの　カウボーイで　ゆうめいです。



Cowboys appeared in the late 1800s as workers who drove large herds of cattle from Texas to markets in the US and Mexico.
１８００ねんの こうはんに　 カウボーイは　テキサスから　アメリカと　メキシコの　いちばに、うしの　たいぐんを　つれてきました。



They were young men aged 12 to 18, available for seasonal work to help herd the cattle thousands of miles to their destination.
かれらは １０００キロもの　もくてきちまで　うしの　たいぐんを　ていきてきに　つれて　行くことが　できるのは　１２さいから １８さいの　わかものたちでした。



The journeys were usually boring, as they spent weeks on horses walking across the plains.
へいげんを　いくしゅうも　うまで　いどうする　そのたびは　たいくつなものでした。



Sometimes the rides were punctuated with dangerous stampedes due to lightning or dangerous river crossings.
ときどき　そのたびは　きけんな　川　わたりや　いなずまで　うおうさおうして　だいこんらんになり　ちゅうだん しなければ　ならない　ときも　ありました。



By the 1890s, barbed wire fences and other regulations forced cowboys to give up their trade,
as railroads became the primary method of transporting cattle.
1890ねんだいになると　ゆうしてっせんの　かこいや　カウボーイに　ぎむかされた　きそくで　ぼうえきが
できなくなり　うしの　いどうしゅだんに　てつどうを　つかうように　なりました。



Houston is famous for NASA’s Johnson Space Center, referred to as “Houston” by astronauts as they orbit the planet.
ヒューストンは、うちゅうひこうしたちが　じんこうえいせいで　きどうに　のるときに、　
”ヒューストン”と　さけぶ、　ナサの　ジョンソンうちゅうセンターとして　ゆうめいです。



There are no launches from Houston;
ヒューストンには、はっしゃだいは　ありません。



shuttles and other US spacecraft are launched primarily from Florida.
シャトルや　ほかのアメリカのうちゅうせんは、おもに　フロリダから　はっしゃされます。



The Johnson Space Center is a 45 minute drive south of Houston, and is a popular tourist trap.
ジョンソンうちゅうセンターは、ヒューストンの　みなみ４５ふんの　ところにあり、　かんこうちと　して　にんきが　あります。



Austin is the capital of Texas. People in Austin call it the music capital of the world.
オースティンは　テキサスの　しゅとで、　　オースティンの　ひとびとは　せかいの　おんがくの　まちと　よんでいます。



If you come to Texas, I hope you have fun.
みなさん、テキサスに　くることが　あったら　ぜひ　たのしんで　ください。
