PulpRevelation

By Rob Nugen

Pulp Fiction revelation

10:40am Sunday 11 March 2001

Wow!

For the past 30 or so rallies, my mugbook quote has been a line from Pulp Fiction. This has been true for all but like 4 rallies out of 30.

While registering for Plano Rally, I was scanning through the script to find a line. I found in the opening scene, when they decide to rob the coffee shop, the woman's line is "I'm ready, let's go, right here, right now."

However, that's what's written in the script; I want the line she actually says. I put in the VHS and was watching the scene when I heard the most amazing thing.

(This is for trivia buffs or Pulp Fiction junkies only)

When she says, "I bet you could cut down on the hero factor in a place like this."

In the background, Jules says, "Well yeah! I was eatin' my muffin, drinkin' my coffee, an' replayin' the incident in my mind, when I had what alcoholics refer to as, 'a moment of clarity.'"

So perfect! Just as she says "no hero," our hero speaks up in the background. Awesome.
