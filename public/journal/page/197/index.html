Right now I”m on the bus and スマホゾ had to be told by the bus driver to move to the back of the bus. I wonder where it will lead in the future. Will there be ubiquitous personal locomotion techniques that allow complete sensory immersion in the devices so the real world can be ignored? Will there be legislation passed that limits the use of such devices while in self-guided motion? Will there be violence between the factions of real-worlders and personal-worlders?
