##15:56 Wednesday 17 December 2014 JST##

Sweet this Limited Express train is fun to ride as it whizzes past like 7 stations between KQ Kawasaki and Yokohama (sorry, Travis). I might try to ride in the very front (passenger) seat next time. It features big windows to see out front.

To be honest, I’ve never really liked riding trains from KQ Kawasaki; there are several lines which use the same set of platforms so I can’t just jump on any train that comes by. Plus the trains range from local (and hit every station) to limited express (and skip most stations), so even if I’m on the right line, I might take “forever” to get there or miss the station completely. fucking Finder

By Rob Nugen

##21:56 Wednesday 17 December 2014 JST##

More than once in recent memory, Finder has hung when trying to load images. It responds in every other way, but simply refuses to display the files in the Finder window where they should be displayed. Why?

It’s (fortunately) not the (SSD) drive dying; Emacs loads the list of files immediately, but Finder just hangs.

Even Terminal displays them instantly.

And it’s not like there’s a million files. In these cases, there are six (6) and two (2) files, respectively, less than 2MB each.
