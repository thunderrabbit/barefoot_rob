dream wearing shoes in public

By Rob Nugen

05:06 Monday 28 May 2018 JST

Walking outside, I realized I was wearing hiking boots and was shocked that I was wearing them while walking around on vacation. I asked store owner if I could have a bag to put them in, but he said he could not give me one, but I could not understand the reason why. I walked further and found a place to put my boots in a locker so I did that and saw Yumi there who taking things out of her locker and she said I could put my shoes with her stuff with kinda the implication that her son would be able to wear them when he got older. That's fine with me and I was glad to be barefoot again, but I wondered if I would really never need them in the next 15 years; I do wear them in winter sometimes!

Dear Congressman Ted Poe

By Rob Nugen

08:49 Saturday 26 May 2018 JST

Please support Net Neutrality. Otherwise, you are going to have to pay to get your message out to constituents.

In short, I want you and all your fellow member of congress to vote the opposite of whatever Ajit Pai suggests. As you can see on the ArsTechnica(1) website, Pai’s so-called “light-touch approach” of the previous 20 years actually consisted of much heavier regulation than exists today.

I personally have multiple websites that are free for users to read. I cannot afford to pay higher rates to allow my sites to compete with the likes of Amazon, Facebook, Google, or other well-established firms.

We entrust you with the ability to vote on many topics, most of which I do not understand, nor care to understand. However, in this case, I understand quite well, and hope you will vote with 75% of Americans wishes (2). I know not all Americans are your constituents, but I cannot find a survey that polls just this district.

(1) https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2018/03/ajit-pai-says-net-neutrality-was-the-top-threat-to-broadband-deployment/ (2) https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/the-switch/wp/2017/12/12/this-poll-gave-americans-a-detailed-case-for-and-against-the-fccs-net-neutrality-plan-the-reaction-among-republicans-was-striking/
