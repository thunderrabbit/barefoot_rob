State of My Life Address

By Rob Nugen

17:22 Thursday 08 August 2019 JST
  1. My old thunderrabbit router conked out so now we are using a new one with three antennas and mutiple channels and all that good stuff.

  3. This allows me to scan A4 pages and upload to my b.robnugen.com server (b stands for binary). Most of them are going to be in https://b.robnugen.com/robot/ (where robot represents for R.O.B.O.T., which stands for my personal religion I made after reading Shaving the Inside of your Skull by Mel Ash) whereas others will be in other directories
