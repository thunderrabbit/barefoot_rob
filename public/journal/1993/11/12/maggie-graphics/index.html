Maggie graphics

By Rob Nugen

(transcribed from Book Six 8:38pm JST Saturday 20 June 2009 (day 14332))

12:40AM Saturday 13 November 1993 (Day 50)

"Today" Maggie came over for a while & read while I worked on graphics. I am entirely rewriting my graphics assignment. It is not too terribly big compared to Database, and it will be much better written if I rewrite it.

I worked on graphics _all day_ & then I went roller blading by myself. I cruised down to the Marble Slab area by River Oaks & then back down W Gray to Cecil's bar via Niecy's house, but I didn't knock on her dor. I got water at Cecil's & then cruised home.
