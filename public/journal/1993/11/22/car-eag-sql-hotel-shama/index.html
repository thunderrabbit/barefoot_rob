22 Nov 1993, 00:00

car, EAG, SQL, Hotel Shama

By Rob Nugen

(transcribed from Book Six 4:34pm JST Friday 26 June 2009 (day 14338))

1:10 AM Tuesday 23 November 1993 (Day 60)

"Today" my car stalled on the I-10 feeder in the right turn lane to turn onto Silber.

I alone pushed it around the corner & up over the railroad tracks while barefoot. It was quite a challenge. It wouldn't start until I propped open the butterfly valve on the carburetor.

Maggie & I watched the Simpsons that she had recorded. I had seen it.

Automata sucked.

Today I wore long pants because I figured it would be cold. It was beautiful sunny but it got cold when it got dark.

EAG meeting with Stephen Barth was today. I had never met him before. We figured out how to volunteer for buildings & stuff, and brainstormed on what to do in the future.

After he cruised, Shenan requested a title within the group so she took Geoff's title of vice president. I got a title. Now I'm the Recycling Chair of EAG. Bad Ass.

At one point Shenan was talking & I was getting up or something and she said, "Sit down," in a false grouchy voice. So, I moved my chair right beside hers & sat down right beside her. Touching her - shoulder to shoulder. She smiled, "smart ass." At one point as she was talking I flirtatiously banged my knee against hers & she banged back. Cool. She is really a babe.

I think I should try to be friends with her in the same way that I became friends with Laura W.

Database sucked.. Near the end of lecture I woke up and someone had written WAKE UP really big on my paper. Heh. I love it.

Lab was okay. I flirted with Eva but I think she didn't take it as flirting, but just put up with it as an annoyance.

I'm getting this SQL shit figured out.

Me, Molly, Maggie, Will, John & Lisa ate at the Towers. I had the guy blop a big blop of sloppy joe meat on my pizza so I could smuggle it out. That was a significant amount of extra food.

Lisa had 3 bites off my ice cream bar. yum.

At home I did some SQL Plus & put some tables & data in my thing.

I tutored Heather & got 11 bucks.

I called Cathy & chatted just for a bit.

Shama called & I named an SQL hotel after her.

Good night.
