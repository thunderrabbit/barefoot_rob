Christine frisbee Fred today

By Rob Nugen

(transcribed from Book Six 8:25pm JST Saturday 20 June 2009 (day 14332))

12:40AM Friday 12 November 1993 (Day 49)

"Today" I went to graphics and then met Christine and we talked out by her car.

Apparently she still really loves me and I really touched her & Nathan's lives. Nathan now knows he has feelings and can understand them and act upon them. Christine can now sleep when & where she needs to instead of only in the perfect environment.

Cool. She is worried that she will never find someone as cool as me ever again. Dude.

After gymnastics I played Frisbee with Rita and Keisha while Wasseim talked to Tanya. Then Wasseim drove me to my car & I cruised home. I'm feeling somewhat sick so I slept for 2 hours & then accidentally skipped the Simpsons and I wanted to get a lot of my graphics asignment done but I didn't get a lot of work done on it.

However, Fred & I used our similar voices to trick Caryl (my dad's girlfriend) and it was pretty hilarious. Suzanne feels bad since Lori is moved and I helped her feel better.

Goodnight -
