Grapics Castle of Dreams

By Rob Nugen

(transcribed from Book Six 8:43pm JST Saturday 20 June 2009 (day 14332))

4:35AM Sunday 14 November 1993 (Day 51)

Yesterday I worked on graphics all day and I got excellent work done. I have rewritten the entire project and got the thing working as it should have for the first project. It now uses doubly linked lists everywhere and the code overall is much more flexible & more tightly & consistently written.

Molly came over for a while yesterday and worked on Numerical Methods & napped in my blue recliner.

My project is really kick butt now. The object names are like "dave" & "molly" instead of 1 & 2.

I am really pleased. Oh- and it is not restricted to objects with a certain the same number of points in each face. So I can have objects like

with no problem. Very cool.

I did Castle of Dreams stuff for a couple hours. Scout told me there are 188 Billion ways the first 4 moves (4 by black 4 by white) of a chess game can occur. 1 X 10^116 ways a 40 move game can occur. Dude.

I would like to be able to play chess.

There is a giant food fight going on in the Castle and Elsbeth asked me my ral name.

Jack Dracula is hanging out with us and is his old self again. And Tammy is back as The Hobbit.

Christine & Suzanne came over and we talked and had a standing tickle fight in the hall. We were friends and that was cool.

I slept then woke up at 4:35 AM in a fit of coughing. I'm kinda sick. Bleah.
