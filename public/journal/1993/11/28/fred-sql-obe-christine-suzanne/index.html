Fred, SQL, OBE, Christine, Suzanne

By Rob Nugen

(transcribed from Book Six 10:58pm JST Saturday 27 June 2009 (day 14339))

I see my hands. Therefore I am dreaming.

2:35AM Monday 29 November 1993 [Day 66]

Friday I picked up dad from the airport and stayed overnight at his house. Late Friday night Fred came in and neither dad nor I had seen him in a while so that was cool.

Fred & I played SILVER, a VGA bad ass pinball game for a couple hours.

Then he went to bed & I stayed up till 5AM Saturday morning to beat Fred's score.

Saturday I mostly played around but I also rotated my tires to get my new tires on the front of the car. The one on the front right was in really shitty condition. It's now my spare.

I went home Saturday night with _Journeys Out of the Body_ by Robert A. Monroe. I read almost half of it (instead of doing SQL) and I ended up quite scared out of my wits. So I called Forest & talked to him for a while until I calmed down to sleep.

"Today" I woke up & worked on SQL a teensy bit & then I went to Molly's room and worked more.

Then everyone but I left and I got some good work done on Automata.

Then Christine called & said that she & Suzanne had brought food to Social Work for me. Excellent! So I cruised over there to eat.

I played with them - tickle fought & talked about Out of Body Experiences (OOBE) & Suzanne has done it once & Christine has almost done it but she got scared.

I walked them to Suzanne's car & flirted with them out there. I was getting too horny for Christine to keep me happy so I cruised.

I worked on SQL at Social Work till midnight when they closed. I walked with Shama (originally 3rd Taub now 1st Taub) outside & rode with her security escort to Taub.

Molly & Sabita & Will were outside checking out the lunar eclipse.

We watched then I cruised.

I went to Suzanne's & explained my no sex rules & stuff & now here I am about to see my hands therefore I am dreaming.
