Hung out with Sarupapa and Sarumama

By Rob Nugen

22:38 Saturday 26 December 2015 JST

Lin and I joined Sarupapa and Sarumama at an event in Stopover Tokyo, between 外苑前駅 and 表参道駅. Several clothing designers were there, looking all fashionable / weird. I showed five of my drawings, most of which were unnamed and not necessarily finished. I offered to give them to anyone who liked them. Ended up giving away two: White Dots to Veero (not Vwero), and 東京路線 (which had had a working title of Three or More Circles) to Madoka and Mitsuro.

I asked Lin to write the kanji on 東京路線, but after seeing it written, I wished I had tried doing it myself.
