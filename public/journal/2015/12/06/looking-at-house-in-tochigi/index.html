Looking at house in Tochigi

By Rob Nugen

12:23 Sunday 06 December 2015 JST

We’re on the train now. It’s apparently a 9 minute walk from 藤岡駅. 68 minutes on Tokaido-Utsunomiya line and then 16 minutes on the Tobu-Nikko line or something.

15:30

Cute old house with a bit of a yard in front and nice neighbors on one side. The road out front is a bit too trafficked for Jennie to stay alive very long, and the train tracks are near enough that we can hear the RR crossing bell while inside the house.

The local station is just 5 minutes walk away. Trains pass about once every 5 minutes, but stop at there once every 30 minutes.
