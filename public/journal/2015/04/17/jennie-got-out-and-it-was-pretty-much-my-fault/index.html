Jennie got out and it was pretty much my fault

By Rob Nugen

12:10 Friday 17 April 2015 JST

As Lin left this morning, she was like, “don’t let Jennie out!” and I was like, “of course!” and I noticed that I could put the chain on the door so it only opens this much she wouldn’t be able to get out. That way she’ll be safe and happy!

oh shit she getting out! I tried to close the door to carefully hold her without crushing her (cats hate that!) and I had her at her hips. But then she just twisted her back legs sideways and was like, “bye!”

fuck

I had to fully close the door in order to unlock the chain, and by that time she was 5 yards away, which is like 5 yard more than needed for her to bolt quickly enough that I’d never catch her.

Fuck

So I’ve locked the chain again and propped the door open with my shoe in hopes she’ll come back before Lin does!
