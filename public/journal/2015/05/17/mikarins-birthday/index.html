Mikarin's birthday

By Rob Nugen

20:30 Sunday 17 May 2015 JST

Headed home from Mikarin’s birthday party. Lin’s driving her uncle’s minivan with me, her mom, aunt, and uncle.

Mikarin played a nice song on piano and then was like, 自分で作りました、”I wrote it.” Wow! I wonder if she can/should copyright it.

She and Takun did a magic show for us, including a couple tricks I wasn’t able to figure out.

Takun tried one trick that he wasn’t able to figure out; the die kept showing the same number. Afterward I tried to explain that he should hold the thing like this and not like that, but I’m not sure if he got it.
