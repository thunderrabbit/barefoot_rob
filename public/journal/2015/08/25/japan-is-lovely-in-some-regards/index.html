Japan is lovely in some regards

By Rob Nugen

21:14 Tuesday 25 August 2015 JST

I bought a carton of peach tea and a carton of milk tea. I drank the milk tea and left peach tea on the table, in public, but out of sight of the shop staff.

Two+ hours later, I came back and it was still on the table.

I mean, it’s not like Grand Central, but there’s a fair stream of people who come through here. Thank you, Japanese culture; I appreciate you leaving my tea for me.
