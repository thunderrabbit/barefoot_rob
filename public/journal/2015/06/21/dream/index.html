Dream

By Rob Nugen

20:18 Sunday 21 June 2015 JST

Two types of monsters existed in the city which had existed for thousands of years and recently we realized they had found a weakness in our defenses. We had secured some way that disallowed them from climbing up this set of buildings but they had found a way to beat that with a new building which had been built recently, so they could climb up the new buildng above the defenses and the swing across to our set of buildings.

A small porcupine monster came up through a hole in the sidewalk and was spewing pencils upward; I used some of the pencils to attack it, stabbing downward through the hole so he couldn’t come up.

He asked if I stabbed him 562 times with 562 pencils how many stabs that would be, and I said just 562, one per pencil. I closed the hole with a rock and believed he couldn’t get up past it, given all his injuries.

I went up to the house and told one housemate the problem with the monsters being able to reach the city and that the roommate shoud be careful. A few more roommates came in and I told them the same thing, though one didn’t trust me for some reason.

I left the house and was in a field watching some designs on the ground which I realized were zombie traps, specifically to catch white paint zombies. Basically they were grills in the ground where white paint zombies would pour through the grill and not be able to get back up. The pattern expanded to catch more zombie types until it was simply a large hole in the groud with a place for bait in the middle to attract the zombies. One zombie came toward the hole as my friend was asking me what the hole was for and I said “come with me on this side of the hole and keep the zombie directly across from us and the zombile will walk into he hole and become trapped. Now of course we have to be careful because when we stand over on this side we are very close to the trees so they can get us from behind. plus the hole is only that deep, so it can’t hold like 10 zombies; maybe only 2 or 3.”

Another zombie came and we led it to drop into the hole, but then as I left, the zombie climbed out and said thank you. I told my friend I had never heard them speak before, so I wasn’t sure what that was. Then I remembered MJGs book and thought I wonder if they’re safe now, and asked my friend if they were safe, and he guessed they might be. I was like, Okay you go first” and he laughed. I went back toward the zombie who had been able to climb out and I tentatively went up to him all, um, so , um hi.” and he spoke normally with me, and told me about the time we had gone to a workshop together, and I hadn’t understood the symbology (obviously) but they guy had.

He showed me some symbols I had apparently seen before and said I had missed the obvious clues which he had mentioned. This pattern meant write. So I was able to read, but not to write.

This portion means travel and this means somethign else . Some of the concepts were being translated poorly as through Japanese and I said I think I understand. He pointed out “6 of 25” and asked what it means.. “well 6 means F and twenty five is near the end of the alphabet, so Zombie?” and wondered if he had gone through a progression of stages culminating at Zombie, and had reached pure bliss at this stage.. He spent a lot of time trying to explain the symbols, which were done in a step by step mathematics now that I look at it, and he explained that the maker had been waiting for people to crack the code and said that our little questions had been wasting his (our) time.

At the end, he was trying to communicate next steps and gave me a phone number which I was hard-pressed to enter into my phone. he was saying 090 and I was typing 090 I thought but the numbers all came out wrong, I was getting grouchy but stayed cool and just explained that I didn’t know why they weren’t working correctlly. Then I realized that I was deleting other people’s phone numbers while I was tyring to edit this phone number.

I tried again and again and nearly got to the end of the number which had more than 4 digits in the last part, then I realized he was saying S2

space space space space it was some sort of pager wait two seconds for it to pick up and then send the mesage to the pager, and that was how I could communicate my desire to hear the next part of the message.

I thanked him and hugged him and thanked him for his time, looking carfully into his eyes for a period of time, but not knowing if I’d be able to use his informatin since I hand’t been able to enter it into my phone.
