Watched movie Inside Out

By Rob Nugen

18:10 Wednesday 14 October 2015 JST

Inside Out is the latest Pixar film I’ve seen. It hilariously and perfectly describes how emotions seem to work in our minds. Emotions in balance lead to a normal life, while unchecked emotion of any persuasion can lead to a bit of a disaster. I love how it answers so many unasked questions! “Ohhh! This explains why that happens!!”

Anyway, go see it. I hope to see it with Lin sometime. It’s actually a reasonably good introduction into the idea of emotional growth.
