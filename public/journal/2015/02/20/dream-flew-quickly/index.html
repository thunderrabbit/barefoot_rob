Dream flew quickly

By Rob Nugen

##08:43 Friday 20 February 2015 JST##

(dream)
In a large house with many rooms, a woman was kinda stalking me, telling me she wanted me to give her a baby; I was like no way and trying to escape her, through different interconnected rooms and department store displays. I ran down a long hallway and downstairs to where my sane friends were outside and I realized I could fly, so I flew upwards and wondered if I could fly quickly this time, and found that I could. Some high tension power lines were there, but I was able to quickly fly over them and away.
