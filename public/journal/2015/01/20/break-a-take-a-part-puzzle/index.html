Break a take-a-part puzzle

By Rob Nugen

##18:42 Tuesday 20 January 2015 JST##

I went to the toy store today and they presented me with a puzzle they had been unable to take apart. It was like a “secret box” puzzle, but was not hollow inside. I set my timer for 30 minutes to make sure I left in time for Mutsumi’s lesson, then went to work.

I was able to solve it in about 25 minutes.

The guy saw that I had done it and came over to investigate; I showed him how it went together. While doing so, I dropped one of the pieces and it came unglued! D’oh!! That’s gonna be hard to put back together so precisely, but they (of course) said it was no problem.

I considered buying it (out of guilt) because I had planned to buy it if I hadn’t been able to solve it before having to go to the lesson..

But I didn’t buy it, nor did I take a picture of it (for Fred: hey, here’s what I didn’t buy for you).
