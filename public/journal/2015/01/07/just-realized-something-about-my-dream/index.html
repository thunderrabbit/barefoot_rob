Just realized something about my dream

By Rob Nugen

##07:55 Wednesday 07 January 2015 JST##

I honestly didn’t know what the white limestone was called, but I found it with a search on Google for white stone with shells impressions

I was debating whether I should waste Andrew’s time telling him about my dream when I realized one of the key things I saw was the limestone steps, and the first image I found which clearly shows them is at dcfossils.org. “DC” as in Washington DC. Andrew is running for US President… wow!
