Pop Pop Chaos can be played anonymously

By Rob Nugen

13:54 Friday 04 September 2015 JST

Posted at https://forums.meteor.com/t/new-easy-silly-game-pop-pop-chaos-my-first-mmo/9295

If you have time to click click click some colorful bubbles, check out my new game

https://www.poppopchaos.com/

Click bubbles to inflate them; the one who pops the bubble gets the points!

Reach the leaderboard by creating bubbles with your hard earned points!

Thanks to https://github.com/brettle/meteor-accounts-deluxe, you can play anonymously!
