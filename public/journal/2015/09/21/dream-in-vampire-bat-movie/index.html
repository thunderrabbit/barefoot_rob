Dream in vampire bat movie

By Rob Nugen

07:01 Monday 21 September 2015 JST

Living inside a QT movie, therewas a spooky scene and then a couple guys were walking along the beach with the task of digging up a dead body. One of them goes, "okay start digging, and the other was like where? and then the first one fell down a bit of a cliff and revealed her corpse, and he goes, "right there" so they dug her out and she was actually still alive, though animated with spooky spirit ghost or something and the card overlay said "Despite her looking like a nyph, this scene is quite scary"

So while watching, I was prepared to turn it off or walk away, but it never got scary too much. There were two undead women teaching me how to fight and they didn't rip my face off or anything and I was thankful for that. In fact, they seemed helpful and nurturing. I felt a bit relaxed as we had a big sparring thing and then as we relaxed I saw a clock count down a random series of digits and then reach the end. I go, "what's that??" and was a bit afraid again. One of the women said she didn't know, and then we saw a bunch of bats flying to surround our glass building.

Uh oh. We coudl see them all smoofing around outside, so we turned off the light inside but I recalled that bats could see infrared and therefore our heat signatures. So we turned off the light and then could see that some of them were inside anyway, and the two girls who had helped me sacrificed themselves to attack the bats, but it wasn't enough as there were thousands of bats, so I tried to tattack them a bit, but bailed due to ineffectiveness.

One of the well dressed vampire bat men with slick backed blonde hair bit my finger with a smile as I flew in to kill him. I realized it was fruitless so I bailed to a new scene in which I was in San Francisco back in the day when it was stil active Alcatraz. We looked out at the island, but there were lots of islands in between and I was sure I could get out to it, or a way from it.. A three times retired army general (served for 60 years) said he would swim out there with me. He jumped straight down over concrete right next to teh ocean and sdove feet first directly down into the water just inces from the concrete. I saw him in the deep water, propelling himself forward with just his feet kicking as his arms trailed back. He made god time and I jumped forward so as to not be so close to the concrete. I wasn't straight as I hit the water and so didn't go very deep into it There were some other boats in hthe waster of some people bothering us, and he suggested we go over to a room blocked by a grinder that was stopped so he went past it, and then I saw him (now little kid) on the other side as the wheel was starting again. He had been giving a piece of paper to take back through the tube and I willed the grinder to not move so he'd be okay. Then some other people tried to come through, starting with an old man, but I couldn't control the grinder as it started to move so I woke up.
