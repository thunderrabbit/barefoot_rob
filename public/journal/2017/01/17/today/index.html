13:00 Tuesday 17 January 2017 JST

At the GG lesson I learned a couple interesting things about Japanese school system.

According to my students, there is a strict rule about how birthdates correspond to what year of school a student is in.

For kindergarten, all students who were born between March 31st of one year and March 30th of the next year will start kindergarten this year.

For elementary school, all students who were born between April 2nd of one year and April 1st of the next year will start school this year.

Wait what? So students born on March 31st or April 1st have to wait in limbo for a year????
