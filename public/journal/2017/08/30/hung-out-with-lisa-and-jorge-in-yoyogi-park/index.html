Hung out with Lisa and Jorge in Yoyogi Park

By Rob Nugen

01:43 Wednesday 30 August 2017 JST

Lisa arrived first and she and I chatted until Jorge arrived. Kinda like a life-sized ouija board, we found a perfect spot in the grass beyond the lakes. Sat and chatted for four hours! Such a nice evening getting to know them better.
