Finally sent book back to Margaret

By Rob Nugen

16:32 Tuesday 11 July 2017 JST

I finally got Margaret’s book on its way back to her. She lent it to me years ago.. like 8 years and said she would like to have it back sometime.. and then our group ended and she left the country.

Funny that after I reached her and got her address yesterday, I suddenly had the desire to read the book to my GG class. I thought they would get a kick out of it, but actually… I was hoping to get a kick out of being such a good guy for sending the book back and reading such an interesting book to the class. Fuck that. It’s Margaret’s book and she apparently has a 7 year old waiting to have it read. Sent it today.
