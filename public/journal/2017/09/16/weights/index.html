weights

By Rob Nugen

18:50 Saturday 16 September 2017 JST

Simon gave me a set of weights, including one which he found discarded on a hiking trail. We hiked up the trail, found the weight still there, collected it and cleaned it up in his shower.

He wanted to give me dumbells, but after I asked what would be good for my lower back, he suggested a T-bar kettlebell.
