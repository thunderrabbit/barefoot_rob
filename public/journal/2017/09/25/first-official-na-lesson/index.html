first official NA lesson

By Rob Nugen

10:14 Monday 25 September 2017 JST

NA is the two letter designation for a new lesson I will be teaching on Mondays. It is within walking distance of the Monday morning lesson, but there is a bit of a gap in between so I am chilling at a restaurant in Tokyo Tower.

15:35 Monday 25 September 2017 JST

Lesson was tough at first when I tried terribly to explain my intention to the group of quietly patient students. Man oh man they had a bilingual woman last time; how I wished she had been in attendance!

Got it sorted out after I started doing role plays, in which, fortunately, I was able to incorporate some of the words we had gone over before. Yay for winging it!

In other news; it looks like a boat race place is being built in Roppongi! Ain’t water for miles around but the signs at a construction site were all about boat racing.
