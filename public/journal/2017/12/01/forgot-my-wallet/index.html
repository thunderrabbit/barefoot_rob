forgot my wallet

By Rob Nugen

13:16 Friday 01 December 2017 JST

Well! I discovered something new about Tokyo trains. If I promise to pay at the other end, it’s possible to get a free entry ticket. I did not think to take a picture of the ticket, but its kanji started with 無 and may have started with 無理 but I am not sure I can readily identify the second kanji.

Anyway, I called Ty and he met me at his station to let me out. And gave me 1000 yen so I can get home and maybe get a bite to eat.

Headed to Paola’s house now to enjoy lunch and laugh about leaving wallets at home!
