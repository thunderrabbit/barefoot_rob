typhoony morning but trains were fine

By Rob Nugen

14:40 Monday 23 October 2017 JST

When I took the train this morning at 6:24am, it was running on time no worries. However, apparently trains started running late soon thereafter. One of my students happens to live near my same station (新百合ヶ丘) and she said the trains were late, which caused her to arrive late. I am thankful for my policy of arriving 20+ minutes early.
