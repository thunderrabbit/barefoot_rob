blarg stupid paperclips

By Rob Nugen

19:40 Friday 27 October 2017 JST

Playing the stupid paperclip game cost me $50. Okay, maybe I am stupid instead of the game, but due to its clever design, I waited to the last minute to get my train (for a substitute lesson in a location with which I am unfamiliar). My train ended up being late, I missed(*) my next connection, and the client said “screw it” and cancelled the lesson.

(*) got on the wrong line

Now I am back home and dammit the paperclip game is still noxious.

I closed the tabs (had it running in FF and Chrome) and wonder where 40 minutes went.
