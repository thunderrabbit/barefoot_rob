Silent Football love

By Rob Nugen

10:59pm JST Thursday 17 July 2003

Wow.

I feel blessed and loved. I got an email from Ben, asking for my recommendation on a new possible move in Silent Football (ironic since Fred and I were recently discussing how neither he nor I could just arbitrarily make new rules).. Basically defining how/if an outward facing person can Fwap the ball.

And Maggie wrote me a super long email transcribing some of my old-school journal entries (20 March 1993) and has expressed that she really misses me as well.

Sigh. So much love. Thank you, thank you. I miss you guys so much.
