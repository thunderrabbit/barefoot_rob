Homestay registration

By Rob Nugen

11:24am JST Wednesday 30 July 2003

Shin helped me apply to stay with a host family, but we got stuck when I said I wanted to stay for 5 months (mid-October to March).

Or maybe it was that I said I'm a Nova instructor. Those dumb Nova instructors, always messing it up for the rest of us.

Anyway, whereas, per my understanding via Shin, previously, they were like, "yeah you can kickit for 3 months with one fam and then 3 months with another fam," soon as I wanted 2 months and 3 months, I was encouraged to do a homevisit, where I meet with the peeps one day or one night.

Seems that some people had used the families just as cheap housing and didn't really chat with them / share ideas, whatever. So a homevisit is just yammering for a day. Less time spent overall, but likely more chatter time. But I wonder if the chatter would be helpful toward learning Japanese.

Hmmm. So another option is to just meet some of the cats in my apartment slash apply for homevisits in my area and see what happens. Hmmm.
