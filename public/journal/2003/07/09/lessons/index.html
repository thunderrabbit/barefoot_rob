lessons

By Rob Nugen

2:06pm JST Wednesday 9 July 2003

I can't overtly claim to have learned lessons today, but I have learned some things that may or may not apply anywhere else in the fabric of life:

Running down wet concrete steps, while not dangerous in itself, may indicate that a slick tile floor below has been made more slick.

The pain of a headache is distinct from the pain of an overt head trauma.

Though I left the apartment in a rush, my angels guided me back to my lesson with Aki, which I had completely forgotten. I arrived 2 minutes before the scheduled time to meet.

late
