Ma & Jon

By Rob Nugen

3:12pm CST Monday 24 March 2003

"I better say something meritable so Rob will write it down."

"What a world where the leading rapper is white, the leading golfer is black, the French think Americans are arrogant, and the Germans don't want to go to war."

So GREAT to see my mom again.

I told them what I knew about my apartment, my city, my roommates, etc etc. Yammered a bit about the stupidity of war and the awesomeness of Macs.

At first I was like "erg" and didn't want to drive all the way to Waco just for lunch, but something about being so frustrated by the phone made me really really really appreciate seeing ma. Gave her a bigger hug than ever because of that phone.

She brought a pile of my clothes - lotsa rally t-shirts and many ties and I don't know what else. I thought there might be a suit in there but I don't see it. No worries; I only need one.

5:13pm

I'm home now. _8 Mile_ blasting on stereo; house is empty of people and so clean!!!
