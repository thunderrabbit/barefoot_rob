Drive to Waco

By Rob Nugen

10:31am CST Monday 24 March 2003

I'm going to see my mom in Waco (1.5 hours away) today! And... oh she's bringing me some ties (I hope she got the message; I archived some ties with her when I began my homeless life) and I bought a few ties at Ross.

Oh and I gotta pack! And get dollars changed to yen. Lotsa dollars!! Lots more yen!!!

But..... I don't leave until Wednesday...

Aw yeah.. I've got plenty of time.

12:18pm

See? I'm already in the restaurant 45 minutes early. I like being early, but I don't necessarily need to be *this* early to be happy. Maybe 10 minutes. But hey, plenty of time to write. Maybe I'll compose my first mass email that I plan to send.
