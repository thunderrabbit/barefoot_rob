Genius

By Rob Nugen

7:43am CST Wednesday 5 March 2003

This morning I asked Janette if she would superglue the flap of dead skin to the bottom of my foot (onto the new skin that can handle superglue, but not a rock or small piece of glass). She did and when I got up I decided to not move my foot for a bit and then fall down when I tried to move with my foot "stuck" to the floor.

While I stood, waiting for the perfect moment for my joke, Janette was all, "I asked Josephine what she thought about me dating you," and blah blah "then Josephine said, 'you've been acting like a couple of teenagers for five months now. But whatever makes you happy.'"

I started to crack up thinking about my joke; I could hardly finish my tea. Finally I got my poker face and then "I better go to work"

[step]

[fall]

[BAM] against the refrigerator as I fell.

HAHAHAHAhahahahahahahhahaha!! I'm so glad Janette and I share our wonderful sense of humor!

"... and then I, a genius at balancing, suddenly fall...."

- - - -

I've wondered about the term genius being applied to things other than mental abilities (artistic, emotional, physical, spiritual)

7:52am

Now I really gotta go to work.
