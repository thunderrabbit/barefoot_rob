Swa'vet

By Rob Nugen

11:41pm CST Tuesday 25 March 2003

Whooo! Just got a call from Swa'vet who I met at Kinko's while she helped produce Dirty Third II back in like May or so. Wow!! So great to talk to her again and she might come see me in Japan in like November or so! How awesome would that be!

She's worked on two more movies, one similar to "Ghost" and one called Panther about a female super hero. I don't remember much more than that.

I told Swa'vet the story of me meeting her and Juni and Greg and Steve at Kinko's and then me and Fred seeing her et. al. about a week later when she cracked me and Fred up, "stop fuckin' around and call me tomorrow!"

Didn't tell her the part about meeting Wissam and oh man I forgot his name and he was the one who wrote me back! Something with a C.

11:46pm

Back to packing!
