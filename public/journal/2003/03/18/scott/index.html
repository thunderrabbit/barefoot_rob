Scott

By Rob Nugen

4:34pm CST Tuesday 18 March 2003

Have just gotten back from applying for Josephine's passport and transferring Scott's title to Janette. Effectively to Josephine but she doesn't have a driver's license yet. Stuff like that.

On the way out of the Dallas Records building (Courthouse?) I saw a news truck so I asked the guy in the truck what was the big news.

"There's a water fountain in there that says 'whites only'"

Holy shit.

He downplayed it as worthy news, but I'm thinking dammmmmmmmm.
