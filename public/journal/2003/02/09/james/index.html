James

By Rob Nugen

8:35am HST Sunday 9 February 2003

Met a cool cat named James and his girl (didn't get her name) on the shuttle bus from Waikiki area to the airport. They were en route to check out the Arizona; they had seen the Missouri yesterday.

James drives trucks across the country in 2.5 days from New York City (where he grew up) to LA. Takes different routes depending on the weather. He's seen the white buffalo in Colorado, which Janette assures him is good luck. "It gives you good luck and good looks."

He really pimped Germain's Luau, saying it was really pimp and his girl had gone parasailing which was quite fun, especially when the boat driver would slow down and let her feet dip into the water. Crazy!
