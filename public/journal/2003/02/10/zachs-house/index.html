Zachs house

By Rob Nugen

6:36pm HST Monday 10 February 2003

Now we're at someone's house in the middle of forest.

- - - -

Met Djinn Jah and Ashira, who are on the fourth day of a ten day fast. They might not finish all ten days as this is their first cleansing fast. Master cleanse, I think they said.

They are living on 3.4 acres of land that some cat owns and is building a house on it. Zach lives there too, in a secluded tent tucked away in a bamboo forest. So awesome.
