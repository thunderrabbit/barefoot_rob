Josephine shower

By Rob Nugen

6:16pm CST Thursday 2 January 2003

Josephine went to take a shower and Heather needed to get something for whatever reason.

Heather was all, "Josephine? Are you in the shower?"

"Yes!" Jo hollered through the door.

"Can I come in for a second?"

"Yeah."

Five seconds after she went in a closed the door, I went up to it, "Heather? Are you in the shower?"

"No!"

"Can I come in?" "just kidding."

Then Mikey and I both went up to the door while Heather and Josephine were still in there. Mikey goes, "Rob? Are you in the shower?"

me: "Yeah!" them: "No!"

"Can I come in?"

me: "Sure!" them: WTF??

us: Hahahahhaahaahaahah! and walked away.
