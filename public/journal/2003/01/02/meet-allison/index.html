meet Allison

By Rob Nugen

10:47pm CST Thursday 2 January 2003

Allison, Sean's ex girlfriend happened to be at the house when we all made it back. She seems like quite an outgoing person, living fully happily in New York (Janette says she (Allison) saw the WTC get fucked up in 2001), going to school at some artsy school.

This summer she'll visit her brother in the Ukraine, and get to do something (go to Lucca) in Italy and then work in Italy as a photographer for her friend.

I didn't really talk to her as she was hanging out, but she left to see her boyfriend (a performer in the band Polyphonic Spree (is that the one that someone and someone else drove like a really long way just to see? I don't remember, but it sounds close (I don't think it is)) which is apparently performing tonight) I was all, "you have good energy," and "I'm glad you're going to do cool things this summer."
