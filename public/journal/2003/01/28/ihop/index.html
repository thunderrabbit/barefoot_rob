IHOP

By Rob Nugen

12:34am CST Wednesday 29 January 2003

Went to IHOP with Joel, Kate, Josephine tonight. Mostly Joel and I said "Alright" like Mitch Hedberg.

The waitress gave me Josephine's whipped cream from her hot chocolate. She was not willing to make a face different than a happy face for Joel's Smiley Face pancake. But she did give him the less expensive adult Smiley Face pancake instead of children's version.

We switched tables while Kate was in the restroom, but she didn't notice.

Tina called Kate's cellphone and I answered it, claiming it was my cellphone, then gave it to Joel, who talked a bit, then gave it to Josephine who did not get to talk because Tina had hung up. Then Tina wouldn't answer her phone when Kate tried to call her back. I hope Tina is okay.

I climbed into the back hatch of the van, over all the seats to the driver's seat to drive home.
