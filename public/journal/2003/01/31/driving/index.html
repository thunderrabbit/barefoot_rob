Driving

By Rob Nugen

9:22am CST Friday 31 January 2003

Before going to Houston I'll take Jack by the movie rental place to hook a brotha up with a movie for him to watch while everyone else is gone.

10:32am

Okay. I've showered, vaguely packed, remained calm. Happy thoughts.

10:46am

Jack made me a vegan sandwich for the road! So awesome. *And* gave me chips *and* a coveted orange Sunkist. MMmmmmmm

11:34am

I just remembered that I forgot the two tiins of cookies Janette gave me to take to the party. D'oh! but it's cool; we'll just have more cookies to enjoy at home. 38 miles traveled so far.

12:05pm

72.1 miles traveled. I'm trying a new technique to make the trek to Houston feel shorter: I'm stopping somewhat frequently. I'm at a rest area now, and will visit the next one in 91 miles.

12:10pm

Driving again.

1:26pm

OOps. I just passed the next rest area. I saw the sign for it and then started engaging my brain again and realized I forgot my mouthguard. That thought process sucked up enough braincells to make me forget all about the rest area. 168 miles driven now. I'll stop in Huntsville or so.

1:32pm

Oops. I missed the exit in Huntsville that I intended to take. Okay, here's another. If I can keep this thought in my mind for 2 more seconds.

Did it. Whew.

175.55 miles driven.

1:40pm

They didn't have Reese's Pieces! I don't know what's up with that.

2:24pm

Freeway is blocked with a bit of construction. From what I can see there's a cat helping to guide a rather large frame thing that will eventually be used to hold an exit sign or something.

223.2 miles traveled.

2:53pm

Have just arrived at Maggie's work. 249 miles traveled, not counting the 3 or so erased when I reset the odometer a bit after I left, synchronizing it with 156000 miles on Scott.
