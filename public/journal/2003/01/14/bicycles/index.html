Bicycles

By Rob Nugen

4:46pm CST Tuesday 14 January 2003

Janette and I rode bikes to the bank, the library, and the grocery store. From the library we got _Stranger in a Strange Land_ so I can continue reading it to her. From grocery we got spaghetti making items for dinner tonight.

Before the ride, something was bugging her; I don't know if it was me or not. She felt some better during the ride, but not 100% better as she went back out alone to the park.

I hope she'll talk to me soon if it was something I did.
