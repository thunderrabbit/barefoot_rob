NOVA

By Rob Nugen

8:50am CST Tuesday 21 January 2003

Went to Kinko's, got the copies I needed of my passport and printed out my resume. Now I'm at a postal place to ship off my stuff to Nova with overnight delivery. Zooooom!

Been listening to Sam Malone on 104 KRBE. Might call in tomorrow early and tell him I've been on a road trip and going to Japan and see if I can get a hookup on some good stuff. SEEya!

9:09am

Whoooohooooo!!

I have just sent off my documents to Nova with overnight delivery to arrive by 10am tomorrow. FedEX tracking number 838441258096 so I can watch it zoom over.

I asked the cat if they had an option to get it there today. They used to before World Trade Center got hit. It was called Sonic Delivery. For only a few or several hundred dollars, Whooooosh!
