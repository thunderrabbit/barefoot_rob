Transcribing

By Rob Nugen

1:55pm CST Tuesday 21 January 2003

I have been transcribing some old entries from 1987, when I first started my journal. Interesting to see my use of the word "you" when I meant "people in general." I used the term "messed around" or "messin' around" a lot. And lots of apostrophes instead of writin' a g.

Also interesting to see me as a youth through my stories. Chowing even more ice cream than I do now. Losing clothing and toiletries during a camping trip. Physically stronger than I am now. Some sadness that I haven't kept up that level of physique, but hey whatever. I am who I am.

They haven't been moved online yet, but someday soon they will be.
