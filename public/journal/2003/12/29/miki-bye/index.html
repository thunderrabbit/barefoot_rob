Miki bye

By Rob Nugen

11:34am JST Monday 29 December 2003

Miki fired herself by not being willing to try going to Farm Grill in Ginza. I can't hang with someone who chooses to be so helpless. I will however, still give her the art thing I made for her. I closed it today; I was going to draw about twice as much, but it suddenly appeared finished after I saw it again today.

11:47pm

I want to be more specific about this. She said, "I can't go to Ginza because I have never been there before and I can't read a map."
