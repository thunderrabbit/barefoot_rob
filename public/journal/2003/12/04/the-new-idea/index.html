The new idea

By Rob Nugen

9:02am JST Thursday 4 December 2003

The old idea was to save Japan from itself. Fuck Japan. (*) I need this for myself.

(*) Let Japan live at it has. From Jon:

I don't know when you wrote this, maybe it was early, but by now

underlying core of their culture is intact, based on their warrior
code, which dates back to the 13th century.
