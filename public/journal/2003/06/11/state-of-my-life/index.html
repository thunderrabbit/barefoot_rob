By Rob Nugen

8:59am JST Wednesday 11 June 2003

State of my Life

  1. I’ve been in Japan for nearly two and a half months.

  2. I’ve just started teaching private students this week. The first was fumi, who I helped for 2 hours in Shibuya yesterday.

  3. Second and third will be Aki this afternoon in Tsunashima and Shin tonight in Kikuna.

  4. Between tutoring Aki and Shin, I’m going to see The Matrix Reloaded with Cory and Janis in Kawasaki! It’ll be my first time to attend a movie in Japan.

  5. I went through a two week period of not studying Japanese well at all. I’m focusing myself back toward studying.

  6. There are 25 email messages in my inbox. I’ve been working toward keeping that number down (by responding to messages I’ve received instead of just letting them rot).

  7. Haven’t found an official Mankind Project group in Tokyo, but when Frank gets back in town, I’ll ask him if he knows of any (through AA).
