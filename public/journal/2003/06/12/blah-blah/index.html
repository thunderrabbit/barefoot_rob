Blah blah

By Rob Nugen

9:55am JST Thursday 12 June 2003

Rain is a fine misty sprinkle barely perceptible. Tiny little dots appear on my glasses. I woogie my hand through my buzzed hair and no water runs down my face or neck. Not enough water to form a single drop.

Woke up without an alarm this morning (an alarm is rare for me here; I've used mine maybe 10 times, and I think all those times were before early May), though I was a bit groggy after being awake so late online and yammering with janette.

Shower seems to be collecting mildew again. I may scrub it down again. The rainy season is apparently good for mildew growth.

11:52am

Helped Aki learn how to give directions today. "Go this way (point) two blocks. Turn right and go to the second light. You'll see the park on your left."

I wrote for her instructions on how to get to Kawasaki Nova (how to get to the station, take the train, take the next train, etc etc)

Gave her homework: how to get to Kawasaki Nova without using the trains. She prefers taking the bus.
