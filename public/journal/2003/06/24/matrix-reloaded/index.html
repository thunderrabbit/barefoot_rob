matrix reloaded

By Rob Nugen

12:20am JST Wednesday 25 June 2003

Saw Matrix Reloaded again tonight. I got to the theater a bit late, which tends to bug me, but I'm mostly over it. It was worth missing a tiny bit of the movie to get a hug from Fumi.

So.

Matrix Reloaded kicked ass the second time, too. I understood more of the obtuse dialogue, and a couple more of the links between scenes became apparent. I did not however have a huge A-HA! experience upon second viewing.

In a couple of scenes, I focused on slightly different parts. Like watching the truck's front tire instead of Morpheus and the Key-Maker.

I might go see it again.

I'm definitely going to see more movies in Japan, and I'll probably allow more time for getting to the theater.
