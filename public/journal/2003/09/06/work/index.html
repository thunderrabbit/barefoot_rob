Work

By Rob Nugen

9:41am JST Saturday 6 September 2003

Today is the first Saturday for me to work at KQ. Last month I worked there on Mondays and Fridays. This week it's Saturdays, which are super busy at Kawasaki.

今日は　はじめての　同様日に　けいきゅうで　はたらきます。

今日は　けいきゅうで　はじめて　土曜日に　はたらきます。

今日は　けいきゅうで　はじめて　土曜日に　はたらきました。

6:41pm

Today was my first day to work at KQ on Saturday. Kinda busy, but nothin' like Kawasaki. I had one free lesson during which I handed out some Nova Usagi tissues and played Marble Blast. The graphics are so good (or so fucked up) on the game that it made me a little urpy.

I'm on a train now to Shinagawa and then will zip over to Shinjuku to meet with Yumi at Gateway 21.

いま　じでんしゃで　しながわに　行きます　それから　しんじゅくに　ケートウィー２１で　由美と会います。
