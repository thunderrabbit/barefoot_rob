full tub

By Rob Nugen

8:38pm JST Sunday 28 September 2003

The bath/shower room in my apartment is cramped, but has a cool feature. The shower can spray in the bathtub *or* all over the floor and no one minds.

janette likes to take baths. She filled the tub most of the way. When I got in, the water went nearly to the top of the tub. We splashed like little kids and yelled, "you kids stop splashing water!" "Josephine! I mean it!"

"Mom! She splashed water all over the floor!" "No I didn't. It was his fault!"

I lifted myself partway out of the tub and turned the water on until it was brimming and turned it off.

"Ready?"

I was surprised that she didn't see this next part coming. I sank down into the water, causing a tsunami over the edge of the tub. janette giggled like when I say something funny.

"I'm telling mom."
