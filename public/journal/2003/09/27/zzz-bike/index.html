zzz bike

By Rob Nugen

2:02am JST Sunday 28 September 2003

Wooooo

Awesome. Put my bike together tonight and rode around with janette. Saw Jon M (who is living with Olivia and Jen for his last few days in Japan) on the way out of Bell Wistaria. My bike felt weird at first, but by the end of the evening, while janette was stuck in a median waiting for highway traffic to go by, I zoomed up over and down the highway on the pedestrian ramp thing.

Supazoomed down the hill into Tarumachi. It ruled. I'm *really* happy to have my bike here in Japan.
