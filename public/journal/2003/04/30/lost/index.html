Lost

By Rob Nugen

5:49pm JST Wednesday 30 April 2003

Dammit. I knew that cat was giving me incorrect directions. At the bike place, after getting my bike back, and profuse verbal thanks given to Koko's mom, Koko asked where Tsunashima was and he pointed all over the place except where I knew the shit to be. I went his way anyway and now I'm 5km from Shin-Yokohama and 1km away from Hiyoshi and dammit if I can't find Hiyoshi on the map to get an estimate of where I am.

And double dammit that I don't know where it is; cause I should.

I'm debating going back the way I came to prove that I coulda gotten home without his help,or had I ignored his help but fuck that wouldn't prove it to anyone but me.

I'm worth it. I'm going back.

7:01pm

We were both right. I had missed a turnoff.
