Bank

By Rob Nugen

11:48am JST Thursday 10 April 2003

Whoohoo! I just got my bank card here in Japan. Now I just gotta find which ATMs I can use. Then I just gotta get my first paycheck on 15 May.

I called my US bank and they said they can't request a funds transfer from my Japanese bank. Benny has said we have to wire them, or maybe he said we could wire them but there's a cheaper way; I forget; I'll ask him.
