April fools

By Rob Nugen

7:59am JST Tuesday 1 April 2003

No foolin' it's been a week that I've not had my computer online. Hopefully soon I'll get that rectified.

I had dreams last night including a scene where we were all in a room for a class of some sort and Michael Jackson (the new Michael Jackson) was outside the window and people were taunting him. I was like, "hey, listen. That cat worked his ass off and made a million jillion kazillion dollars. Just because he's changed doesn't mean it's appropriate to gawk at him and treat him like an animal." or something like that.

Most recent dream included Tammy and Christie from my late high school days as we were remembering all the good times and fun games we had played like The Seven Game and Silent Football. (in 'reality' we did play the seven game, but not SF)

So I have a few hours off from work this morning to clean my room that's still not perfectly organized, and has actually gotten a little bit fucked up with all my Nova shit dumped everywhere on the floor as I'm sorting through it all. Part of it I want to archive in a scrap book which I don't have yet, and part of it I might need for future reference, and part of it is just trash to be chunked. Er, recycled.
