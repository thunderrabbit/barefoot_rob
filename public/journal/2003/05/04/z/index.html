Z

By Rob Nugen

10:47pm JST Sunday 4 May 2003

Instead of going to get my bike tire fixed, I studied a bit and fell asleep a lot. Dreams filled with the concept of learning Japanese, trying to learn Japanese, bits and pieces of words floating around in my head.

Woke up naturally just before Frank yelled "WHAT???" in the other room and then the washing machine beeped its 10-beep "I'm finished" alarm.

In my head I wondered "what brings you joy, Frank?"

Soon as I got into the room he was all, "I gotta ask you a favor; when I lose I get really angry." He's playing Sega basketball.

"So what's the favor?"

"Just don't comment on it. Your predecessor had to learn the hard way."

"Did you kick his ass?" I ask, thinking the question is ludicrous.

"No; I very rarely resort to physical violence."

Oh, well that's nice.

The .... apparent accuracy of this video game is pretty amazing. Taking into consideration number of fans the various teams draw, and using two announcer's voices, a voice for the local announcer, players' voices, tennis shoe squeaks, buzzer horns, advertising in the arena... wow. Pretty far cry from Adventure for the Atari 2600, which I think I could still play happily. Maybe 1 game on the most difficult level.
