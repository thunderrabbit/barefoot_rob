Today

By Rob Nugen

12:53am JST Tuesday 13 May 2003

Today at work everything went pretty well. I'm at that stage where I'm just basically there, doing my thing - not in super brain-overload processing mode.

Talked to Janette who said the earthquake was reported to be 4.3 or 5.1, depending on the news source. She asked about my new gaijin card and I told her I'll get it in a couple days. So I was looking for the uber-important document to getting my gaijin card: my passport. Right now, it seems to be missing. I'm not freaked yet, but I will destroy my room tonight until I find it.

1:06am

room status: intact

passport: found

me: relieved

It was in the correct place almost. I had put it in my undershirt drawer instead of my underwear drawer.
