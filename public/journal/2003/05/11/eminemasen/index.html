eminemasen

By Rob Nugen

8:36pm JST Sunday 11 May 2003

Welp, unless something quite surprising happens, I won't be attending either of the Eminem concerts in Japan this month. Just today two things happened:

(one) Jon offered to work any Friday for me.

(two) I was told I have training on the Friday of the first Eminem concert.

I don't know that I could get out of the training since I normally work on Fridays, but.... it sure seemed like an interesting cusp of possibilities.

I got to work early and Masahiro, Yuki, Sumika all helped me with my Japanese skillz. I helped Masahiro with a couple of English words.

Happy mother's day to all you mothers and your mothers.
