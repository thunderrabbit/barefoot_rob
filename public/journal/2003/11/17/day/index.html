Day

By Rob Nugen

2:02pm JST Tuesday 18 November 2003

Yesterday I cried as soon as my mom left my field of vision. That's when it hit me. I'm alone again in Japan. I wondered why no tears were coming as we hugged and hugged then stood there in Shinagawa station.

I still don't know why they didn't come then, but at least it's not cause my tear ducts are broken or something like that.

- - - -

Okay. I'm not going to write a whole lot, but we went to Kamakura and that was nice. Saw a shinto wedding and went through the small cave with tons of buddhas in it (and I'm getting dejavu on that experience now, can't quite place it) and went inside the daibutsu, but we couldn't go up to the top (roped off).
