i-group

By Rob Nugen

9:30pm CDT Monday 24 July 2000

Tonight I surprised myself in I-group. I felt disconnected from everything, so I presented my feelings to the group and quickly found myself in tears that I miss Wende “so much, and it’s only been 2 days.”

The core was fear that I wouldn’t be able to take care of myself without her around. I then recognized that I was hungry, and hadn’t eaten dinner. So I checked out of the group and came home to eat.

Read some Java In a Nutshell book, and now I’m going to bed.
