i-group

By Rob Nugen

9pm Monday 10 July 2000

In my men’s group tonight, I expressed an inability to relax, because I think I must do something in order to be of value for the world.

The fouled logic goes:
If I don’t do something, then I’m not productive
If I’m not productive, then I’m not helping society
If I’m not helping society, then I might as well not be here

I recognize the value to my soul of relaxation / meditative time, so I set the intention to relax for 1 hour… on Thursday.

I will call or email Dan to tell him of my success.
