keyboard!!

By Rob Nugen

1:10pm Thursday 06 July 2000

Wow!!!

This is pretty frikkin’ fly! I just received my stowaway keyboard for my Handspring Visor! I can now write journal entries so crazy super fast anywhere I go! It fold into fourths and fits into my pocket!

It’s a bit weird to have the keyboard slightly flexing when I hit keys on the near the ends of the keyboard, like backspace and shift, but I totally love it!

For example, whereas it would normally have taken me like 15 minutes to type all this text, it’s only taken me about 2 or 3.

Sweet!!
