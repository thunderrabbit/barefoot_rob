By Rob Nugen

not much work today

9:04pm CDT Tuesday 11 July 2000

I didn’t work a whole lot today; I was significantly sidetracked with watching stock prices and discovering the source of an error in my investment club accounting. The stocks didn’t move much. The error was minor.

I recognized the need to purchase the accounting software from NAIC (National Association of Investment Clubs).. I’m sure their software does everything my spreadsheet calculations do and more!

I reviewed two CDs for KTRU; I’ve had them out since early April.. And I still have 1 more CD checked out for review! I’ll do it soon.

For dinner, Wende came to my work with Taco Bell food and it was so so yummy! I had only a sandwich during the day, and some leftover meatloaf stuff and potatoes and green beans for breakfast.

I turned in my two reviewed CDs to KTRU and now I’m home!
