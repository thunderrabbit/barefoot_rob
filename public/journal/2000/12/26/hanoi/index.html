9:00pm CST Tuesday 26 December 2000

emacs moved the eighteenth ring!! Now it will take this many days again before it gets to move the nineteenth ring.

I just used dc to determine that it will take approximately 5.845 * 10^11 years before it finishes. I think I already wrote that, but not as precisely.

Today at work I did some more flash stuff - I made a simple form that sends variables to SAS. Now I have to figure out how to send variables back to the shockwave movie to make it work as a survey they way I want.

So far, I have not found any tutorials on the web that describe how to do this.

23:48 Tuesday 26 December 2000

Good news: I have found out what I need to make the Flash stuff work the way I want.

Bad news: I typed on the emacs hanoi window, causing it to stop.
