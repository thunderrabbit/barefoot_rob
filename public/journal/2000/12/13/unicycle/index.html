unicycle

By Rob Nugen

4:05pm Wednesday 13 December 2000

Wow.

I just unicycled from my work parking lot down 1 long block to the street with the bridge with the tube through which I crawled this summer. I fell a few times on the way there, mostly when going up big bumps in the sidewalk, as tree roots have lifted and broken the sidewalk over the years.

Each place I fell (except for one), I moved the uni back several feet and tried again until I made it past the bump. The one place where I didn't try this was a huge bump about 10 inches tall. That's almost half the height of my wheel.

But! The most amazing thing is that I unied back all the way along the same sidewalk without falling, including down that big 10 inch drop! I was super impressed that I made it down the drop on my first try. I just slowed down before it and then [shoop], down the incline and continued on my way.

I have unied at about this time in the afternoon each day this week, and a couple days last week. It's the perfect break for me.
