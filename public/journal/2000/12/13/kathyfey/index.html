By Rob Nugen

Missed Kathy Fey appointment

7:17pm CST Wednesday 13 December 2000

Grrrr.

I just got home from a productive day at work and discovered that I had missed a massage appointment with Kathy Fey at 6:30. AARrggh!!!

See, the sucky thing is I really have felt all stiff in my neck and been thinking that I need to go see her sometime, but I thought I must not have set an appointment because my Visor alarm didn't go off.

Turns out I stupidly didn't set the alarm on my Visor for this appointment. Grrrrr!

I called Kathy and told her that I would pay the full price of the massage; that way I remain in integrity with her. She offered that I should just pay half.

When she asked about setting an appointment for next week, I balked. Finally I told her what was in my head: Wende and I have been griping about money, and I've wasted some and will just have to effectively spend 1.5 times the normal amount for 1 massage.

Fighting about money is so stupid. Why do we do it?

I want to "talk to Wende about it," whatever that may help and tell Kathy tomorrow what we determined.

Ironically, Kathy has an open house tomorrow and Wende has the day off. Maybe we'll go visit Kathy tomorrow then.

7:25pm

I have just now called Wende and we will go to the open house tomorrow.
