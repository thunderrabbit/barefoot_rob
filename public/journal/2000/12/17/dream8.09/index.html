Dream8.09

By Rob Nugen

Both of you!

8:09am CST Sunday 17 December 2000

(dream)

I had a tiny little radio transmitter and a tiny battery and it was so cool that they could be hooked together to make a radio!

On a bus, but I asked my bus driver something and then got off the bus, and when I tried to get back on the bus, she had alreday left. So I got back on a different bus; they were all going to the same place for a school function, and I just needed to get there. The bus driver gave me a little flack: "You can't do anything or I'll kick you both off."

"Both?"

"You and your wife." She indicated the girl standing beside me (whom I had never seen before; I didn't even know she was behind me.)

"Um, okay." I figured that I'd help out this random person and let her get on as well without having to plead the bus driver as I had just done.

We arrived at our school function place.... something about people wanting to gohang out somewhere else.. I don't remember exactly.
