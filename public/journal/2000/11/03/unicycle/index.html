By Rob Nugen

Unicycle!

9:32pm CST Friday 3 November 2000

I just unicycled to the bank and back. I fell once on the way there and twice on the way back. On the way back I fell once when I rolled through some water with slimey slime in it. The wheel slipped sideways out from under me.

This time my trek included me crossing five different streets (actually 4 and I crossed 1 twice) and going over the little speed bumps in the movie theater parking lot instead of around them. Yay me!!!

I’m not quite ready to unicycle to work, but soon. Maybe this year if I practice more frequently. I considered going to see Wende at work (and hope she hadn’t left by the time I arrived), but I decided I was too tired.
