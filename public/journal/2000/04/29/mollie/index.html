mollie

By Rob Nugen

9:58am CDT Saturday 29 April 2000

“I’m going to go write a journal entry,” I announced.

Molly goes, “what are you going to call it?”

“I dunno. What do you want me to call it?”

“Are you going to call it ‘spot’ like all the other entries this month?”

“What??” defensively.

“Just teasin’.. only two of them.”

“I’ll call it Molly.” but I secretly misspelled it. Muwahahahah!!!

- - - -

Last night I rode my (borrowed) unicycle again! I didn’t do as well as two days ago, but still was much better than day 1. I actually felt just tired, super low energy as if I hadn’t eaten enough recently. I sat on the curb and waited for Wende to get back from the grocery store. (We had just eaten at Taco Bell and she was going to pick up milk on the way home. She came back with lots of bags full of groceries.)

- - - -

This morning, Will and Molly helped Wende and I get the last last last of Wende’s stuff out of her apartment. She doesn’t have to move out until tomorrow actually, but we’re not that good at procrastinating.

As we were getting the last of the stuff, Rick came out and described his recent adventures as he and his best friend Doyle were mountain biking. Doyle had an impressive accident and had been in ICU for 2 days. He’s getting much better, but is still in the hospital. His story sounds quite similar to my skating accident in 1994.
