sick

By Rob Nugen

11:15am Monday 3 April 2000

I feel a bit headachey and sick today. Just sore throat and an infrequent cough (two coughs in 2 hours that I’ve been at work). But cute was me last night telling Wende in a huge haze of headache pain, “I have a three brain headache.”

The comment seemed normal to me last night. The pain felt like it was taking up 3 heads’ worth of space centered on my head. It was enough to get Wende out of bed to get me some advil and decongestant.

This weekend Plano Rally was super great fun; I was in YAC meetings for many hours.
