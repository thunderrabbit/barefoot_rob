By Rob Nugen

Last Night

6:43am CDT Saturday 15 April 2000

Last night was my last night at my apartment. It stands out from other nights because my cat was a maniac! Meow meow meow meow meow meow meow at 4:00 o’clock in the morning. I think he just wanted to go outside. Poor mr Spot is being converted to an inside cat now.

I tried to tell that cat to chill by petting him, but most of my energy was not inside my body; I kinda just flopped my arm on top of Spot. He wriggled out and had a 4:am meal at his food dish.

So this is it. Moving day. Maggie, Molly, John, Will, Dude, Jason, Wende, Stacey (sp), and I will all be here at different parts of the day to help me and Wende both move; mostly starting in 2 hours.

I’m going to pack my computer now, unless I can think of a good excuse not to.
