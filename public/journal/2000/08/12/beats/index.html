beats

By Rob Nugen

9:44pm Saturday 12 August 2000

Robin and I will perform freestyle beatbox during the talent show which was supposed to start at 9:30. The new Steering Committee is still meeting; we are guessing the talent show will be at more like 10:30.

We just practiced a bit. Nice.

Carissa showed me her Beastie Boys Sounds of Science album, which I had never seen nor listened to. I definitely want to purchase the album now. I love the mix of “Body Movin’” !

I’m considering attending the Youth Advisor Training Training in November. If I attend, I will be called to run youth advisor trainings around the country. Sounds way cool and way youth empowering!
