15 May 2000, 00:00

negativland

By Rob Nugen

4:52pm Monday 15 May 2000

Pooh.

Negativeland concert is tonight. This is good. I’m going to attend. This is also good.

Back when the concert was first announced, I was asked by the KTRU station manager if I could please help them set up for the show. This is good.

I’m at work now, listening to KTRU over the web, and they’re hyping the concert and apparently started setting up at 3pm.

I called the station to say I’ll be there soon as I get off work! … But they don’t need me anymore. Pooh.

I wanted to be needed, but I’m not needed anymore. I called Wende and expressed my bit of sadness to her. She understands. Thanks Wende. :-)

We’ll munch dinner soon and then I’ll zip off to the concert. Wende’s not interested; she thinks Negativeland is not really music. She’s mostly right, but it is art.

See ya!
