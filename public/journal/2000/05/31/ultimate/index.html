By Rob Nugen

Ultimate

10:33pm CDT Wednesday 31 May 2000

Ultimate was great fun tonight! I’m super pleased that for the second week in a row, Wende came out to watch me play! She even bought a collapsable chair so that she may enjoy watching for longer without sitting on the ground or standing the whole time.

My game was great - I had a few great catches, including a couple tall jumping catches that I would not have made in recent history. I made several scoring catches, and threw several scoring throws. Even blocked a couple of other players’ throws.

I came out of the game with only one slight injury: I made a slide-on-the calf catch, scraping my left calf a bit.

Super happy that Wende came out to watch!!!

- - -

Before Ultimate, Wende took me to Best Buy, where I got my 11,000th day present from her: a Handspring Visor. It’s basically a Palm Pilot (it uses the Palm OS). Apparently the guys who made Palm Pilots quit the company to start Handspring. (That’s a rumor Wende heard from someone at her work)

Yay! Now I can write journal entries while away from home and work! Most super-duperly, I’ll be able to write at the OKC rally this weekend. Super great!!!
