This is so funny!

5:28pm CDT Wednesday 10 May 2000

Oh my gosh you have to try this!!!!!!!

Items you need:
Small packet of Pop Rocks candy (or equivalent)
1 cup of tasty beverage (your choice)
1 stethoscope (optional)

What to do:

  1. Put all the Pop Rocks in your mouth
  2. Wash them down with tasty beverage
  3. Listen carefully to your stomach!

I just did this to see what would happen, and I could clearly hear the Pop Rocks popping inside my stomach! And I don’t even have a stethoscope!!

I just wish Wende were here to laugh with me!
