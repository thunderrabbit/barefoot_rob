summer

By Rob Nugen

summertime

7:17am CDT Thursday 18 May 2000

I love summer!! I love the warm weather and I love the sun so early in the morning!! Before 7AM, sunlight streams through our bedroom window, providing warmth and happy energy! Summer is great.

Today I go into work a bit early so we can interview Duane at 9am.

I wanna send The Feel Good Net today... but I can't find my deck of "52 Special Traditions for Family and Friends" I'll look some more this morning. The Feel Good Net is here

- - -

7:58am CDT Thursday 18 May 2000

How very interesting. I asked Wende if she had seen the 52 Tradition Deck, and she had not. Instead she offered one of her family’s traditions, so I typed hers into The Feel Good Net. As soon as I finished, I looked over at on of my desk drawers and opened it. The deck was right there.

Too perfect to be coincidence; it’s called synchronicity.
