By Rob Nugen

Dream

7:10am CST Tuesday 28 March 2000

(transcribed from a dream)

This morning I woke up from a full and complete dream:

Jon Benet Ramsey was being questioned about her parents and their actions and about her actions and just various questions about the living situation in general.

I just happened to be there at the questioning; the meeting was at my church, with a few impartial members asking her things so they could get a better idea about what would be the best course of action. I happened to be there and overheard the whole discussion.

In my men’s group, I’ve been informally trained as a good listener and feedback provider. We each can listen to someone’s story, pick out nuggets or otherwise bring a different light or angle on the situation. I did the same thing in my dream with Jon Benet’s story.

The adults in the meeting began talking amongst themselves, and I got the opportunity to talk with Jon Benet. I told her what I saw from my perspective, and described the best suggestion I had to help her heal the whole thing: forgive those who have hurt her, and mentally bless and love the whole situation.

Please note that I have not once really even listened to or read the whole story as presented by the media. I am not even sure if I’m spelling her name correctly. Back to the dream.

We both had a good cry and I agreed that it could be a difficult thing to forgive, but I believe it’s the only way to heal this wound.

Our conversation ran its entire course naturally with no interruptions. We hugged and said goodbye.

I woke up and recounted the dream. Then I realized, “whoah! wasn’t she murdered?” I wonder if someway somehow our spirits actually had a conversation that manifested itself to me as a dream?
