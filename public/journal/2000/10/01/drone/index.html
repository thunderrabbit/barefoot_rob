Drone

By Rob Nugen

8:48am CDT Sunday 1 October 2000

I don’t know what a swarm of killer bees sounds like, but I can hear the whining drone of the Grand Prix cars racing around a track in downtown Hosuton this morning. In the three years that they’ve been here, I’ve never watched them race. I’ve skated on the track (not during the race) and I’ve even skate up and down some of the stands (bleachers). But never have seen them race.

I even tried to buy tickets last year (for Kristen and I), but my bank account was overdrawn. (I didn’t realize this until I got to the ticket booth.) I took that as a sign to not buy tickets.
