By Rob Nugen

7:15 pm 8 october 2000

I just rode my (borrowed) unicycle over to La Jaliscience to meet the twins and John and Will. I was quite pleased and smiley; I didn’t fall all the way here. “No way dude!” “Yeah. I just now did it.”

Wende just arrived and they all got up. “Oh, you all get up to meet her!” Except for Maggie, “I told Rob I wouldn’t get up for you because I didn’t get up for him.”

7:25 pm

I jumped up and knocked my chair over to hug Melanie when she walked up.

8:04 pm

Wende: “Ooh! I think there’s a mushroom on my plate.” She picked it out with her fork. “Is this a mushroom?”

Maggie held out her hand and Wende wiped the specimen into her hand. Maggie licked it up into her mouth and everyone watched in anticipation.

Suddenly Maggie spat it out and shouted “ewww I don’t know!!”
