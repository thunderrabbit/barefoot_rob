By Rob Nugen

Ultimate

2:04am CST Sunday 13 February 2000

Just finished taking a shower after playing Ultimate. I play barefoot, so they call me Wonderboy on the field. This also means that when I shower, large muddy streams flow from my feet to the drain. Kinda fun for the 5 year old in me. I’m all clean now, though.

I got a surprising email recently from a woman named Dreama. She was doing genealogical research, looking for the middle name of her GGGrandfather. Turns out he is my GGGGrandfather, and she found his name on my Geocities site. Funniest thing is that I didn’t know I had genealogical stuff on my Geocities site!

I’m guessing it was simply a reference to his name from a few years ago when Laura and I were doing lots of research together.

- - - - -

Super exciting news!! I got the days-old counter working on my site! It was already working but only to tell how many days old I am. I wanted for anyone to be able to enter their birthdate, and have it show their age in days! And now, you can do it!

I also updated my webring links. I like the new format for The Spirit Ring! I will probably try to join one more (or more) online journal rings.

With Love
- Rob
