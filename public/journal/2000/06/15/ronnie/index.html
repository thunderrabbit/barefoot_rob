ronnie

By Rob Nugen

7:09pm Thursday 15 June 2000

Rob: I met a guy with an amazing story!
Bryn:: about?
Rob: Four years ago he lived in Palestine. Moved to United States with no English knowledge and $300.
Bryn:: Oh my gosh!!!!
Bryn:: thast sooo cool!
Bryn:: hows he doing now?
Rob: His tuition was paid at San Jacinto college. He got off the plane, into a cab and just pointed to the address.
Bryn:: oh my gosh! how lucky!
Rob: He’s got two jobs and is working his ass off each summer so that he lives off savings for the Fall and Spring semesters at UH, getting a degree in Chemical Engineering
Rob: He sleeps 3 hours per day during the week and sleeps Sunday 2am to Monday 9am
Bryn:: whoa!!!!!
Bryn:: that is nothing!
Bryn:: he must be crazy!
Rob: Totally!
Rob: He appears very happy and I see him every day.
Rob: He is a valet at a restaurant across the street from work.
Bryn:: wow.. that is so cool
Rob: Yes yes.
Rob: I asked why he moved…
Bryn:: he must be an incredibly strong person…
Bryn:: why?
Rob: In Palestine, he’s gotten shot 4 times from age 14 to 18 or so.
Rob: Because of all the war
Rob: They carry guns to school at 12 years old
Rob: The old joke is not so funny cause it’s true… “They pat you down at the door of the school to see if you have a gun. If you don’t have a gun, they give you one.”
Bryn:: oh my gosh…….
Rob: He said, “here, it’s Heaven”
Bryn:: how old is he now?
Rob: Appears to be mid 20s
Bryn:: wow.
Bryn:: that is sooooooo amazing!
Rob: I know!
Rob: I was just all, “Ya know, I cannot even conceive of what you are telling me”
Bryn:: that guy must haev beAn through so much!
Rob: oh yes.
Bryn:: i know!!! all the shootings? man.
Rob: Awman and I forgot to ask his name again. I learned it once, but forgot..
Bryn:: i went to this leadership conference last weekend, and there were two discussion groups about school violence.. but i don’t know anything about it
Rob: Ronnie!
Rob: I think that’s it.
Bryn:: ronnie!
Rob: Well, you just tell them that your main man Ronnie carried an M16 when he was 12.
Bryn:: haha.. thsat so scarry though
Rob: Truue.
Rob: Okay; I’m gonna work some more. Miss Wende is still at work until 10pm.
Rob: I love you lots!
Rob: hey, can I put this conversation on my website?
Bryn:: Absolutely!
Rob: Thanks!
Bryn:: Bye rob!!! Be happy!
Rob: See you soon!
Rob: You too!!
