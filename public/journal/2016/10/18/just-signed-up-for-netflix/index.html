just signed up for Netflix

By Rob Nugen

01:14 Tuesday 18 October 2016 JST

The TV mysteriously broke while I was in OZ and Lin got a new one that supports Netflix, not just Hulu which we’ve liked for a long while, (and others we never used).

Netflix is hella better than Hulu.

  1. can pause movies for a while without the stream being disconnected
  2. can fast forward and reverse with great control
  3. can see thumbnails larger than a postage stamp
  4. can see titles and descriptions in English
  5. can rate movies
  6. can get movie recommendations per different profile
  7. seems like a lot more variety of things to watch
  8. 5% less expensive

Just turned on Lock Stock and Two Smoking Barrels, and realize I’ve never seen it before!
