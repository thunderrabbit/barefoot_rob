permanent resident

By Rob Nugen

22:46 Wednesday 19 October 2016 JST

I now have permanent residency status in Japan.

I don’t really know how I feel about it. Good? Traitor?

After cutting my toe on the way to work, I put some tissues on it and it stopped bleeding without having to tell anyone at work. Yay. I don’t want to be pressured into wearing shoes just because of a little blood.

On the way home I asked Cine where she had been and she said a family member passed just 7 hours ago. I’m about to call her after I get off the train.
