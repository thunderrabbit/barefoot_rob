We rented two movies: Transcendence with Johnny Depp, and San Andreas (Japanese title カリフォルニアダウン) with Dwayne Johnson.

Bought some food at a bakery, stopped by a bag outlet (that’s apparently making enough money to buy the next lot and make bag outlet 2) where we saw some good bags, but none had all the features that I want:

  • roller bag with
  • easily replaceable wheels
  • super strong handles
  • super strong backpack straps
  • computer pocket big enough to hold computer and books (total 10 cm thick)
  • compartment big enough for my big shoes
  • separate compartment for pens and toiletries
  • waterproof

I’m having trouble finding more than 2 or 3 of these requirements in a single bag.

If it could double as a chair and have an awesome guarantee, I could forgo the backpack part, which probably makes it a lot easier to find.

After looking at bags, we went to a chinese restaurant where we got too much food for our own good hahaha.
