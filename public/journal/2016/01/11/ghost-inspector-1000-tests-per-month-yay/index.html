Ghost Inspector 1000 tests per month yay

By Rob Nugen

12:09 Monday 11 January 2016 JST

A few days ago I signed up for Ghost Inspector and thought hmmm I’d love to have 1000 tests per month instead of just 100.

Boom they have that option!

We're glad you're enjoying the product! Unfortunately, we don't
offer any paid plans that are less than 10,000 test
runs/month. However, we do have a promotion with Runscope (our
parent company) where your free account will be bumped up from the
100 test runs per month on the default free plan to 1,000 test
runs per month if you sign up for both services.

I signed up for runscope and set up an API test to boot!
