Chillin with Joanna and Eric in Yokosuka

By Rob Nugen

17:13 Sunday 10 January 2016 JST

After an oopsy trip to Chiba, we went to the station after Yokosuka. Got some Chinese food at a small shop with Chinese proprietors with whom Joanna spoke a bit in Mandarin.

Went to see Eric’s old house in the area, and in the same neighborhood met an Australian guy named Geoffrey who had just moved into his house 9 months ago and has been reforming it since then. Wow! It looked really nice, and he said it’s the third house he’s reformed in this type of way, so Lin and I got his infos to see if we can have him work on our new house, whenever we end up getting it.
