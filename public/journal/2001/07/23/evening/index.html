23 Jul 2001, 00:00

evening

By Rob Nugen

8:44 pm 23 July 2001

Stood with Joana in a super incredibly long long long line for dinner. (Okay, not that long, but I'm harassing Bryn.) We munched and she told me more Germany stories - everyone smokes there (even have smoking areas in the high schools), and they stay up way late even on school nights, because school is only from 8am to 1pm.

Stood around outside and talked randomly and to random people who walked by. I declined playing Capture the Flag. My feet are too sore to want to play.

10:48 pm

We just played a spontaneous game of Silent Football, with Bryn as Madam Dictator. The game was great fun with Bryn's smiley rules. 'Everyone must smile :-)' and we named the universe 'Shiny Happy People Holding Hands'

Anyone who Zoomed a Zoom Zoom had to do jump shake your booty dance as the rest of the group clapped and chanted the song.

Elizabeth Severence lost. I intentionally was picking on her (to speed up the game cause we were late for touchgroups), and then she and I were back and forth with the football and Danny called us on it and we each got 1/2 point, increasing her score to 5 and my score to 4.5

We are in touchgroup are now playing Ceiling Wall Floor, and taunting Mr Sexton, Mr Nathan Sexton. (That was his name in Silent Football.)

As I wrote this entry, I just pulled an "I'm not playing" after the locations were given. I love that.

Actually, more than one person has no fucking idea how to play. ;-)

Ceiling Wall Floor is a sick sick game. :-)

11:22 pm

There has apparently been a crisis; we are about to have an agony meeting.

Someone went into the wrong gender bathroom looking for someone else of the equally wrong gender.

3:10 am 24 July 2001

just finished roving; my & Pat's specific responsibilities for the week are over. Goodnight
