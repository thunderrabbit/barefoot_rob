23 Jul 2001, 00:00

Day 2

By Rob Nugen

23 July 2001

Walking toward opening circle I ate the 3 Oreo cookies off the ground that Bryn and Vessy so carelessly left. When I caught up with them at the circle, Bryn offered me some. I was pleased to be able to say, 'I just had three.'

11:13 am

Sang in the shower.

Oh, I'm takin' a shower for the first time in two days..
It feels oh so good in so many ways
I'm washin' my back and washin' my legs.
I'm gonna go get me some eggs

etc.

Got breakfast which was pancakes, not eggs, and have been chillin' with Tina P, including through the theme speaker, Anita, who makes art.

Intermission now for touchgroup meetings.

3:04 pm

Did the Anita Workshop - art with ink and cloth and stuff. Very fun. Mark's Peter is playing kickball. I'm chillin on TinaP's sheet with TP and Kate.

3:11 pm

AJ just tried to climb the backstop, but the top of it is so rotted that it just splintered when he climbed the fence.

Then he scooped up the pitch, and started chasing Michael A off the field in a most amusing, inappropriate way.

Mostly it was funnier than inappropriate.

6:24 pm

Went swimming after kickball, and played Marco Polo. I was It and the group went underneath the dock. I was all 'Marco!' bonk and the 'Marblublubblub' as I went underwater. Funny.

Chickenfought with David Johnston on my shoulders and beat two other teams. One of them was a rematch. I think Tanner and Adam as a team. The other was Katie Lerner and Ashley Powell.

Just in the past two hours I finished a little round pillow with a peace symbol printed on it. Gave it to Ann, our cook, as a pin cushion. Then made a dream catcher and Tina P was all, 'nononononono you're doing it wrong!' and showed me how to do it. I picked up some of her hints, but added my own style and put it in her mail bag.

Touchgroup meetings now.

7:31 pm

Agony meeting about money. We are all crowded into the dining hall. I am sitting on the floor near the mailbags, with the fan blowing on me.

Dangit; we just had to turn off the fan because we couldn't hear.

Someone stole some stuff.

The SWUUSI staff did a great job of expressing how important this community is, and how anti cool it is to steal money.
