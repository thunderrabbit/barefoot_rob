27 Jul 2001, 00:00

AM

By Rob Nugen

10:58 am July 2001

I slept for about 1.25 hours, down at the end of the road.

Ellen seems to have recovered nicely; she just mentioned that she is going to win the next game of silent football that she plays.

Closing circle/worship was hot as the sun, but nice. We wrote/read blessings from the week.

Hugs to peeps as they are leaving at random moments like photons leaving an excited atom.

Heidi just told me that she has been inspired by me to be a youth advisor. What an incredible blessing.

(note: a few of you have said that, and it's an incredible blessing each tiime. I'm grateful to be here for you.)

- - - -

Bryn walked up to me. She was crying.

"Rob, may I have a hug?"

"Of course, my angel," and I put down = dropped the stuff in my hands and hugged her so tightly for minutes as she cried.

"I've just been losing so many friends this month," referring to her Aussie mates and YRUU homies.

I feel blessed by her emotions.

- - - -

Hugs and tears from Elizabeth, who believes this is her last YRUU experience.

I told her to write me; I will write back. (I'll write back to any of you who write me.)

11:23 am

Chilled until about 12:30 while we determined where to meet Sean & Joey's dad, and where to find Danny's mom.

Sleep time for me.
