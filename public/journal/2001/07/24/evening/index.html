24 Jul 2001, 00:00

evening

By Rob Nugen

2:15 am 25 july 2001

Social Action today was an auction. Amy bought me (for the dance tomorrow) for 10 dollars. I felt wonderfully honored; this was the highest price allowed.

Mark bought himself and Hillary, Chez, Ashley, and Meg. Very funny.

Worship was lovely. I got to sit back to back with Mr The Bibbster. I dropped into the bucket a single grain of sand. Back when I attended the inital program at The Monroe Institute, we got to see the Powers of Ten video. After the video, I went outside and looked at a single grain of sand in the sidewalk and cried.

So, a single grain of sand is just as worthy as any planet or galaxy.

Wrote notes to several peeps and am about to go sleep out by the lake on the asphalt and watch stars and grains of sand.
