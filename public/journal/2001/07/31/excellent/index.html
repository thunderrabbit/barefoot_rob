excellent!

By Rob Nugen

9:26pm Tuesday 31 July 2001

I'm in an excellent mood tonight, which is a welcome change from lately. The reason? --> Wende is open to the idea of us selling our house and taking a bicycle tour around Europe.

I'm not suggesting that we miss a bunch of rallies, but maybe we can squeeze in a tour for a month or two and miss 1 rally. Or maybe we say fuckit and stay longer. Maybe we can actually look at a calendar and not just theorize.

Thing is, she's actually considering it! Thinking about how we can do this thing! I never want to leave advising SWD YRUU, but I also have to follow my dreams, and come back with some pretty awesome stories, -!!And!!- be able to say with confidence to my beloved youth, "you can do it; here's how."

I'm still floating with glee from the incredible time at SWUUSI.. I'm thankful for happy happiness all around!
