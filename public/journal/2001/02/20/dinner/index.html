dinner

By Rob Nugen

7:07 pm Tuesday 20 February 2001

I've just taken a nap after we arrived home from shopping. I had a tremendous headache, but it s relatively gone now. We are about to go eat (!?) though we have eaten pretty recently it seems to me.

It's cool though; we are getting to eat with Michael and Emma; I'm glad to get to see them again.

8:40 pm

I just said "HI" to Emma because she was staring at the visor

I'm writing to say they pronounce the H in herb.

Molly, ask John how long it would take a Concorde to fly from LA to Melbourne, then let me know.

9:45 pm

The ice cream here is dumb. It's more icey than creamy. They actually have three types of "ice cream:"

  • extra creamy
  • ice cream
  • ice confectionery

That's from creamiest to yuckiest. Josephine says that's because they don't want it so fattening. I can see the logic, but I said, "just run (to work it off)."

differences:

  • The cigarette packs have written in large lettering right on the lid of the pack stuf like, "SMOKING CAN KILL YOU" and "SMOKING HARMS THOSE AROUND YOU." and "SMOKING CAUSES CANCER." Lots and lots of people smoke even so.
  • There is a bug zapper right in the kitchen up by the ceiling in this restaurant.
  • restaurants are not allowed to give doggie bags (because people sued after getting food poisoning)
  • They ask "what's the time?" instead of "what time is it." (You can guess what I answer.)
