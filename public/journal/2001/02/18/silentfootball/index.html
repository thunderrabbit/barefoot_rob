SilentFootball

By Rob Nugen

10pm or so 18 February 2001

Bryn and I played the most awesome variation of Silent Football.

The football is moved using the normal rules, except:

  • we can fwap to ourselves and through ourselves (as long as it doesn't go backwards through ourselves). Like I can fwap fwap fwap and it goes from me to Bryn, to me, to Bryn.
  • we can fwap any number of times up to about 60.
  • we can zoom to ourselves.
  • if someone makes an error, the other raises their hand and the one who made the error becomes dictator:
  "Miss Bryn."

  "Mr Dictator, Mr Rob fwapped a fwap fwap."

  "Miss Bryn is correct; Mr Rob receives one 
   one hundredth(*) of a penance point."

  "Mr Rob."

  "Madame Dictator, Miss Bryn is a tattle tale."

  "False. Customary tip of the hat..."

It's tricky when we both raise our hands at the same time.

But so much fun!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

(*) The game ended when I awarded Mr Rob one mole of penance points and we looked it up online to see how a mole is defined. (It's the number of hydrogen atoms there are in one gram of hydrogen (6.023 * 10^23))
