film

By Rob Nugen

2:36 pm

I went shopping for Nick at the Duty Free store:

12 rolls fuji 200 speed  36 exp
1 roll 800 36 exp
1 roll B&W  kodak 36  200 speed

They only had the 12 rolls, no 800 speed nor black and white.

"You understand you won't be able to use it until you leave the country?" he asked.

"Oh yeah." I lied. whatever I thought.

Paid for the film and got the bag, totally sealed and taped and included a warning:

WARNING
$5000 FINE
TAX AND CUSTOMS REGULATIONS APPLY

And goes on to say that I must show this sealed bag to the customs agent along with the sealed receipt attached to the bag.

Hmm.

My passport number is on the bag, and certainly in their computerized system, so when I scan my passport to get out of the country, they will be all, "so, where's the bag?"

Doh!

Oh well; we can use it when we get home.

We are considering if we can unseal the bag and put the film back in and make it look like it remained sealed... I'm really not comfortable with trying to save tax on $69 AUS at a risk of $5000 or 12 months in prison.
