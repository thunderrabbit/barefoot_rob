21 Feb 2001, 00:00

Leaving

By Rob Nugen

9:46 am Wednesday 21 February 2001

Arriving at the airport to leave now.

10:07 am

We have checked our bags through to Los Angeles and are chillin' in a restaurant type place with a view over the tarmac.

I have just finished all my post cards; I wrote about 40 over the course of our vacation, I'm guessing. Wende has written a lovely postcard to her youngest sister, and has begun her second postcard now.

Before we left the house, Wende and I hugged Irene, Nick, Tom, Dot, and Ed off on their 3 week driving adventure up the coast. They are driving to Sydney today.

We have concern for the sanity of those involved; Ed and Dot bicker incessantly. Eww!

10:26 am

I just saw a guy taking apart a pinball machine - getting a ball unstuck or something. He asked "are you from Canada or America?" with his logic being that Canadians say "I'm Canadian, not American."

My favorite thing about the spoken language here is that *everyone* says "yeh" for "yes." And it's not considered rude! Everyone says it to everyone, no matter what age or relationship. Awesome.

My official postcard count is 43 to US, 1 to Canada, 2 to OZ addresses.

11:15 am

Time to go through customs and we are still eating. Gotta go!!!

11:39 am

Now we are in the Gate 4 waiting area. Flight is s'posed to leave in about 35 minutes.

The flight is apparently only 1/2 full. A cranky 6 year old is pouty crying nearby.

11:51 am

Getting on the plane now.

11:58 am Wednesday 21 February 2001

We are on the plane now. I'm switching back to CST. According to the video display, it's 4:57pm (Tuesday 20 February 2001) in Los Angeles, therefore it's 6:57pm in Houston.
