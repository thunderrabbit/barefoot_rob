Game 2

By Rob Nugen

12:33 pm Saturday 17 March 2001

It is officially damn cold.

We are still watching the other teams play. The Murch team who we beat is gettin' whomped this game.

It's 7 - 3 with white team winning.

Oh, I forgot to mention that I bought a cool color changing disk. It's translucent white at night and turns magenta in UV light. Very cool. Sean thinks it's ugly; he likes the more traditional white disc. Sean of course, is unable to embrace new technology.

We are moving to our new field now. Full size field on a football field with much nicer grass on the side and dirt with some big rocks in the middle.

1:27 pm

1 point each team in our second game. I received the disc on their kickoff and quickly got it to Vev or Mark and it was zipped down to Kat for a point.

Then I messed up a throw at their endzone and they got a point.

This point Sean threw to James for our 2nd point. Chadwick made a great defensive play and Sean made a great catch out of the grass. Amazing.

It's 2 - 1 us.

Good D by Tony for a turnover, but we just turned again and now Mark caught their throw and tossed to Vev, to Tony.. Adam slipped in the grass as it was over his head.

Long throw to the endzone and it bounced out of their guy's hand and Bill slapped the shit out of the disc knocking it out of bounds.

Vev handling to Adam who slid and missed it and now their guy slid on his chest to miss it in their endzone.

Vev to Adam to Mark working it down to Adam to Tony 20 yards from our zone. I heard their guy at the 5 count and Tony called timeout. They are saying we come back in on 8 stall.

1:35 pm

Kat just blanketed me so she can read as well. "We gotta keep our journalist warm!"

Time in.

Back to Mark to Vev to Tony to Bill hammer to Vev who barely missed it in the endzone. Good throw Bill.

A valiant effort.

Disc up and turnover to us. Mark is on it to Vev to Sarah but Ollie took Sarah out.

They overthrew toward their endzone.

Bill amazing catch now to Vev.

Roland (their team) caught it.

Mark to Vev and too low in the endzone.

Our endzone their disk.

long throw caught. (but shitty throw) Vev has it to Mark.. Adam almost caught it but was trying to stay in bounds and missed. Great try Adam!

Pick is called.

Disc is tunedover. Adam has it to Ollie to Tony but missed barely. Roland to their guy 1/2 way down. Vev intercepted! Vev to Bill to Adam to Tony to Ollie to Mark tall catch to Vev on the ground to Adam to Sarah long catch for a point!! YAY SARAH!!!!!

3 to 1 us.

1:40 pm

Kat and Sarah caught two of our three points.

They have it, McCleod is blocking and he's a good blocker. they threw it backwards. James was covering both sides of the throw. Now they threw it away. Equipment timeout for Mike M. Steve has it to Tall Dave (El Mano) to Andy to James for point 4. Long perfect forehand too.

YAY JAMES!!

(The throw from McCleod to Dave was unintentional. People were amazed! "Only McCleod could have seen that!" (he didn't))

Nice roll kickoff by Jaems. long throw but they threw it away.

1:44 pm

We have it on their endzone. Mark has it.. to Andy to Mark to Andy to Mike M now they are out of the endzone back to Andy to Mark to Kat to Mark to Andy to Mike to Andy and Kat dove, missed it on the ground. They scored.

4 - 2 us.

Mark has it 1/2 field long to Tony for a point!!!!!!!!

A single throw for a point. Sean was down in the endzone too, but Tony got it.

5 - 2 us.

1:47 pm

Kat is saying she turned her ankle on that fall.

We kickoff to them 3/4 down center of the field. Sean is on disk now Tony now I can't see. They got a point.

5 - 3 us.

I'm going in.

7 - 3 us. and I got the assist! Andy scored one after I took a bonk on Oliver and the ground catching Steve's toss.

I had caught from Ollie and Sarah had a good catch and then she tossed to Mark. Then Ollie to me all the way at the endzone but not in yet. I was well covered and couldn't see Ollie yelling that he was open, but I got it to him somehow for 7th point. Yay!!

1:56 pm

Halftime now.
