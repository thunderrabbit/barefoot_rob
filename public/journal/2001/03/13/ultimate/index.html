ultimate

By Rob Nugen

Ultimate

11:49pm CST Tuesday 13 March 2001

Big fun at ultimate tonight. This is the first time I've played in a pretty long time. It's been so long that I forgot to wear my contacts instead of glasses. It's been so long that when I saw Kim, who has short red hair, I thought it was Kat and that she had recently cut her hair.

I played super well; even if I had been there consistently I would have approved of my skillz tonight.

One cruddy attempt at a catch resulted in the disk slamming into the tip of my middle finger, lifting the first 2 mm or so of fingernail. It bled some and it still hurts, but it's not too terribly bruised.

Got to better meet Tamara, who came out to UH on Saturday night some weeks ago when Madeline also came out to watch. She wore no shoes tonight because she forgot her cleats. When she stepped thoroughly on a burr in the grass she decided it was really time to go home (she had been saying she needed to go home for a while at that point).

This weekend is Centex in Austin. I don't know if I'll go or not. Wende will be out of town for SWUUSI meeting, which makes me lean more toward going to Centex.
