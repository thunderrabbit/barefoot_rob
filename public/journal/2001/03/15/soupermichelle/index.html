souperMichelle

By Rob Nugen

7:49pm Thursday 15 March 2001

Wow! Wende and I are minding our own business at Souper Salad when a lovely familiar face walked up.

"Um, um, um," I stammered, "Michelle!"

"HI!" she smiled.

"I totally had a dream about you within the last month or so!"

She is married and living out in the country with their lovely baby and two dogs and two cats.

Her husband is working with plants and is quite successful and loves it. So great!!

We exchanged information and gave her the short version of our lives.

Oh! This is Michelle whose ex-boyfriend Patrick was in my dream a couple nights ago. The dream where he found Mr Spot.
