Wow

By Rob Nugen

6:17pm CST Tuesday 11 December 2001

I dropped off my check request form to KTRU and corrected an operator log entry that I had forgotten and zipped out of there thinking I had to rush to my 7pm leisure learning class called something like, "how to eat without eating a lot of sugars and flours" or some such. It meets at Whole Foods on Bellaire.

Anyway, I zipped out of KTRU and checked my phone/watch. Ah. I still have a full hour. I realized after I left the campus that I could have cruised back up to the station and hung out with Dan and Chad, but apparently chose not to. I wonder if I would have if they were cute girls?

Maybe. Probably. Who knows?
