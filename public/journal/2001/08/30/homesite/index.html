homesite

By Rob Nugen

9:53pm Thursday 30 August 2001

This is a test entry that I'm writing with Homesite.

Wende says hi.

10:07pm

Now she's going to bed. I was showing her that Netscape 6.1 will store the output of searches in the sidebar for later browsing. Pretty slick, that Netscape 6.1.

Homesite is pretty slick, too. Costs $89 though. We just got it today so Wende can work on her website. I'll give the URL when she's ready to let people see.

I'd like to figure out how to tell it a list of common tags I write in my entries so it can help me write them more quickly.

Oh, I figured out one thing interesting today.

How I'll know I'm living in the future:

  • I'll wear my internet connection and people will be able to IM me at any time (if I want)
  • We'll have a robot that can vaccuum the floors, including going up and down stairs
  • Most houses are equipped with solar cells and can sell electricity to the power company (who will pay the highest amount at that moment)
  • Cars are hovercars
  • Houston has a train system

The first one in the list is what I came up with first, but the rest are all equally valid.

Okay, I'm going to bed. Tulsa Rally tomorrow!!! I've got to pack!
