16 Jun 2001, 00:00

ktru wack

By Rob Nugen

11pm Saturday 16 June 2001

"Worst episode ever."
-Comic Book Guy

I'm pretty tired, but I will describe in not entirely the wrong order the wack things that happened at KTRU today:

  1. A guy kept calling and asked me to read off the song titles on different albums so he could decide which one he wanted to hear and record it.
  2. I played a song for him not realizing it was marked "NO" and it had (at least) the word "fuck" in it.
  3. The computer was SO SLOW that I could hardly type on it.
  4. The computer caps lock and tab keys messed up so it would only tab backwards
  5. The [save] button on the computer quit working so I had to reboot
  6. After rebooting, the computer wouldn't indicate I had typed anything. (I was typing on the keyboard and nothing was being written on the screen)
  7. Finally got it to log in and still had a load of calls while getting caught up on entering everything I had played.
  8. Got crunched for time at the end and had to cram like 8 playlist and 4 alternative tracks into 1 hour.
  9. The front of the mic fell into my lap as I prepared to speak on air the very last time.
  10. Cute Erin had a sub today so I didn't get to see her(*)

(*) this was actually cool because Danny who subbed for Erin knew how to fix the computer. (Reboot the server (yes Windows sucks shit))
