08 Jun 2001, 00:00

flood

By Rob Nugen

7:24 pm 8-6-01

Rain. rain. rain. rain. We left The Woodlands at 6:00pm. We have traveled 8.3 miles. Eight point three. No end in sight of this line of cars.

7:29 pm

8.4 miles.

"If you guys want to get out and stretch your legs, feel free."

hahahahahaha!!

7:36 pm

The youth are discussing some classic Long Jokes.. the Fuck You Clown joke, and The Man In The Black Hat, White Gloves, And Red Cape That Killed My Mom, My Dad, My Seven Brothers And Seven Sisters

7:37 pm

We have just made 8.5 miles.

I'm going to think of another good Long Joke to add to my repertoire

7:47 pm

I announced, "Okay, now we'e gone nine miles."

"Finally!" and "Yay!" came the collective cheer from back.

"Just kiddin'! We're still at 8.5!!!!"

AHHAhahahahahaaha!!!!!

They didn't think it was quite as funny. Really, I don't either.

I just turned the van off.

7:54 pm

Van is still off. I just called 713-CALL-MAP (Motorist Assistance Plan) and they said there are reports of water flooded across the freeway, so Montgomery County Sherrifs have closed the shit off. It's still raining heavily; we may not be going across anytime soon. Bluh.

8:00 pm

I just called Annie, and we moved forward about 40 feet while I was talking to her for a moment. Still at 8.5 miles on the odometer.

Van off again.

8:04 pm

We just moved again and there is a truck driving backwards on the shoulder with his hazards on. Oh, it's an unoffficial truck.

8:06 pm

8.6 miles baby!!!!

8:14 pm

Smom writes:

According to an eyewitness over the radio, water is rushing over the freeway and and a guy is talking on his cell phone over the radio now from the area.

he said there is 4 or 5 feet of wter (over the road??) up to windows in water on some cars.

8:16 pm

8.7 miles already.

8:26 pm

8.8 miles. Reports of road closures all over Houston. I wonder how it will all pan out for us.

8:29 pm

There is a parade of cars suddenly coming southbound on I 45; I don't know where they are coming from. I wonder if the road is opened somehow or what. Maybe it's traffic U turned around..

8:36 pm

One of te KTRH traffic reporters got stuck in a ditch. Shelters are being opened around the city.

8:39 pm

8.8 miles .... I feel sad. I am optimistic yet that we will be able to reach OKC at least by tomorrow. I predict the youth will want to persevere, and get to the rally. It will make a good story.

8:53 pm

8.8 miles! and we are suddenly moving 100 yards. at 5 mph What is happening???????

8.9 now wow we are flying at 2 mph. two.

8:57 pm

9.0 miles!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

how is it that we are moving????

8:58 pm

Susie again:

we went 9 mph for a minute but not much more than that

9:00 pm

Rock just got to the radio station (KTRH) and he is usually on in the morning so he said 'Good morniing' accidentally. silly man, old habits die hard.

9:02 pm

moving again, but not much... 9.1 miles now

9:03 pm

Christina singing Cabaret...Katy singing softly to something playing on her personal CD player

getting off I45 trying something different....getting on 75, going through Conroe..Kids now singing 'Raindrops Keep Falliing...' Ahh, we're moviing, 35 mph

9:22 pm

Rob again.. We have bailed to highway 75 and are at 12.4 miles. stopped for gas.

9:58 pm

Spirits are up; looks like we hae gotten past, using 75. I will call Annie only once we reach I-45 again

10:25 pm

road is clear and damp. no rain. 65 mph. 45.7 miles traveled.

12:54 am Saturday 9 June 2001

Susie has been driving and I've been sleeping. Had a scary jerk awake from a dream when I thought I had fallen asleep while I was driving and I grabbed for the steering wheel, but it wasn't there and I realized I was supposed to be sleepping. quite a relief that was.

We are at 224.0 miles traveled.

The music on the CD player is a welcome relief from the 'usual' stuff, which is 'usually' stuff that I've heard before a million times. Truth is, though, that this muusic is some that I don't care for in general, but it's nice to be uniqe at least. I would still rate it as good enough to play on KTRU. But I'm guessing it's a bit too popular for us to play. I don't know though.

2:07 am

We are well on our way after the most recent gas station stop, where we have discovered that it appears we left our gas money envelope at the previous gas station. I just suddenly got the brain-flash-thought that we donated the money to a charitable cause .. maybe someone whose car flooded out and broke got the money. Or maybe Susie's husband Kevin will be able to recover it in the morning.

2:11 am

About 150 miles to OKC.

We are listening to Jets To Brazil on CD.

2:16 am

Oh - only 126 miles to OKC; we are 2 miles inside the OK border.
