Swordfish sans zapatos

By Rob Nugen

7:44 pm 14-6-01

Aunt Nancy and I just saw Swordfish starring the supa cool John Travolta as a 'terrorist hating zealot' (Nancy) or a 'precise heartless psycho' (Rob). Pretty good movie, I thought; Nancy is thumbs down: too predictable with unecessary violence ('and I don't mind necessary violence') and gratuitous nudity, though Halle looked good.

I say 'wow,' to whoever's gross mind wrote the hostage bombing scene, and the flying was pretty unexpected.

9:29 pm

The guys on KVRX's News Rant show are funny. I'm enious that they get to be all funny and take calls on the air!!!

Going swimming with Sally now.

Oh. before seeing Swordfish, I noticed that I had left my shoes at the training center, 15 miles away. Luckily, they didn't care; I got to watch a movie while barefoot!! Yay Austin!! My kinda town!.

We bought some of the Adidas sandals, which really I have wanted for quite a while so that we could go eat at Chango's, which, by the way, they joked about on The News Rant, and I called them and hopefully was pretty funny on the air.

2:34 am Friday 15 June 2001

Fell asleep listening to Beastie Boys Root Down single after chillin' in the pool and hot tub. going to Stacy's now.
