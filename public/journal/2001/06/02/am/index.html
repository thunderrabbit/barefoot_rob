By Rob Nugen

8:17am Saturday 2 June 2001

This morning Wende read snippets from her book by Gillian Roberts, which she stayed up late last night to finish. Great writing, and quite funny, especially for a sleepy 1 AM brain reading it.

I got an email from Fred today! (This in itself is amazing) Even more exciting is that he has chosen to attend UT in Austin! Wow! Wende says "I'll believe it when he moves in." She's excited, too.

   Date:        Sat, 2 Jun 2001 00:12:05 -0700 (PDT)

Subject:        G!
   From:        Fred

So I got your phone message and your postcard from
Australia;
unfortunately, Stanford has sucked me up and I haven't

stay in touch.  (Also, my messy room sucked up the

about the

  duty free film

In other news (as you may have heard from Dad), I
decided not to
attend Ohio State after all, and am going to go to
UT-Austin next

that.

(inconsistently) ride
about thirty feet before falling.  So pretty soon I'll
be just good

Time to get bakc to work,
