pulp quotes

By Rob Nugen

7:10am CDT Wednesday 27 June 2001

Last night after work, I watched the first half of Pulp Fiction, writing different quotes to use as mugbook quotes at upcoming rallies. :-)

I also went back through all the mugbooks that I have kept over the years (I seem to be missing more than I thought) and wrote down some of the quotes from Pulp Fiction that I've used so far. I know there are more quotes that I've used, but I no longer have those mugbooks. :-(

So, from the first half of the movie, I came up with the following quotes, most of which have no colorful language, and that I think are either funny (esp. taken out of context), or intriguing, or just cool.

  1. So that means there could be up to five guys up there?

  2. Why don't you tell my man Vince here where you got the shit hid at.

  3. That gun goes against the entire idea behind piercing.

  4. This is a seller's market.

  5. It's on the wall by the two African fellows.

  6. And five in there's one two three four five of us.

  7. Well now I'm definitely not gonna tell you cause it's been built up too much.

  8. I gotta know what a five dollar shake tastes like.

  9. I don't think Buddy Holly is much of a waiter.

  10. I don't see Jayne Mansfield; she must have the night off or somethin'.

  11. You seem like a really nice person and I don't want to offend you.

  12. They talk a lot don't they?

  13. I do believe Marsellus, my husband, your boss told you to take me out and do whatever I wanted.

  14. That's a little bit more information than I needed, Vince, but go right ahead.

  15. Don't be rude; drink your drink, but do it quickly.

  16. Are you talking to me on a cellular phone?

  17. Do you know who Marsellus Wallace is?

  18. Alright, what I need is a big fat magic marker.

  19. If you're alright, say something.

  20. What's your thoughts on how to handle this?

  21. Now if you excuse me, I'm gonna go home and have a heart attack.

  22. No, you won't laugh cause it's not funny.

  23. And now, little man, I give the watch to you.

  24. It's time, Butch.

  25. Great. I never thanked you for dinner.

  26. The name is spanish, but I am columbian.

  27. Let's talk about the rich and prosperous Mister Butch.

  28. Keep the light off.

  29. You know what, I was thinking about taking a shower; I'm stinking like a dog over here.

  30. but it ain't gonna be the kinda money that we can live like hogs in the fat house forever.
