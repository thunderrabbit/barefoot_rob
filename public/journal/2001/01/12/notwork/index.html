NotWork

By Rob Nugen

2:17pm Friday 12 January 2001

For about 10 minutes yesterday at work I hopped on one foot instead of walking everywhere I went. Just for fun. It was really tiring so I quit. Karla said I was shaking the whole building.

A few months ago, I found a golf ball in the ocean. A few days ago I cut this golf ball nearly in half, so the halves are connected like a clam shell, or a pac man. Today I used a spoon and peeled off the top rubbery layer of the golf ball. Just for fun.

I'm in quite a happy mood, in anticipation of the rally tonight. A teeny sad thing is the screen on my Handspring Visor has been blinking off randomly, making typing on it weird, as if I'm typing using a strobe light to see. It seems to still work though. Weird.
