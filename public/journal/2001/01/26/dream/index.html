Dream

By Rob Nugen

bolts in Tinkerbell’s paws

6:43am CST Friday 26 January 2001

(dream)

Looking inside an old pachinko machine type thing (*), like I had the front glass open and could see the mechanism behind the scenes, and it was not screwed onto its base all the way. So I unscreewed it all the way and then had to thread the bolts through the webbing of Tinkerbell's front paws. It was painful, but it had to be done I thought.

She complained while I was screwing them in, and once it was finished, I let her go and I could hear her thump thump thump as she walked, and I realized that it wasn't right; she didn't have to have them.

So I tracked her down and found she was trying to chew at them to get them out. I began to remove the threaded bolts, unscrewing unscrewing and it really hurt her this time, but definitely had to be done and I could see her skin being abraided and ripped due to the bolt. Terrible eww.

and I woke up.

(*) I know that's not right, and has a link to a whole 'nother paragraph of dream, but I'm not sure what...

Tinkerbell is (Wende's) our white cat.
