review circuit

By Rob Nugen

Playlist!

This CD totally embodies experimental electronica.

All are // in their own unique way. Preview the first 30 seconds of each song to get an idea of its sound. The liner notes give great background on each piece.

 

1. 2:00 Takashi Harada untitled 1 The instrument Flossie & Unicorns use?

2. 10:20 David Claman '70 reverberations in one's head after a punk show (according to the liner notes, it's supposed to be played loud, so do it!! ;-)

3. 7:32 Pamela Z Geekspeak computer discussion sampled and looped.

4. 15:00 Marc Ainger Dreaming Hills from out of nothing and back to nothing floats an electronic choir.

5. 3:48 Paul Higham Duck Spindle Trilogy looped electronic sampling. Starts and stops then goes then repeats.

6. 14:35 Francis Dhomont Lettre de Sarajevo a lot like Frankenstein Symphony

(soundscape of a spooky spooky amusement park ride)

7. 2:30 after 10? Erik Belgum Life's Big Dream Home Odd spoken word says "Goddamn" at the end

8. 5:50 Steve Wadhams Harley Spoken word about the awesome Hogs

says "hell"

9. 9:00 Dan Moses Schreier Exotic (short version) spy movie theme repeats endlessly. (thank goodness it's the short version)
