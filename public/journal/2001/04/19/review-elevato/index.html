review elevato

By Rob Nugen

MK Ultra

Artist: Elevator label: teenage USA

Title: A taste of complete perspective

Rob Nugen #72

18 April 2001

All songs //(+) recommend: playlist

 

Layers of sound tie the whole album together. Each song has its own signature.

Quite amazing variety. Songs all overlap (except 9 & 10)

 

1. 5:55 Driftwood rain, bottles, birds, church bells intro guitar jam repeated w/ background vox over soundscape

2. 3:22 My Library in the Weeds fem vox then heavy guitar jammin

3. 1:51 Balance crow, electronic helicopter, high hat jazzy riff. m/f vox.

4. 2:50 Oh Traveller Beyond drums by overwound windup monkey

5. 4:43 A Taste of Complete Perspective jammin' electronic bass, smeared vox

6. 3:40 I'm a Radio Station accoustic with ghost electronic waves, a buzzy middle, and birds at the end.

7. 4:07 The Animals fast then slow almost whispered lyrics. Heavy & ominous

8. 3:11 Two peoples Driven heavy guitar with slow bits and faster bits.

9. 2:34 Remember me, Serenity accoustic with speedy hand drums and m/f vox

(silence between track 9 and 10)

10. 5:52 Flying birds, hard rock, light vox, hard rock, light vox, hard rock

11. 3:06 Uncover road-trip accoustic guitar, shaker, whispery voices, odd outro

12. 2:28 The Leaf casio, buzz beat, whispery singing, sudden ending

13. 15:01 A Taste of the Great Beyond wobbly first half w/ some shouted lyrics. Birdsong decorates middle, then rockin' roadtrip with slightly distorted vox. Really long outro of birds by highway then distorts into an echo chamber.
