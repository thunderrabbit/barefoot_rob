robots

By Rob Nugen

11:37pm Monday 9 April 2001

Thanks to the RCFoC for finding this article on robots!

My imagination is piqued; I can hardly conceive of all the cool-ass shit we will have in 5 to 10 years. Wende has already expressed fear, etc about having nanobots; I wonder how she will react to having 1 meter tall robots walking around?

What happens when a vaguely-intelligent robot gets lost? It doesn't know where it lives, but some other human finds it. Just like a stray cat, do we put up signs? "FOUND: L-27 model robot. light green and gray, scratch on left front arm. email ed@aodfldld.com"

What if a human programs a robot to appear lost, but is actually a spy robot, programmed to read bank account information and then infrared-beam it out the house window to the attacker's listening device?

The "lost" robot would never have to be returned to the malicious human; the information could still be ferried out of the house.

Just wondering....
