z disc golf

By Rob Nugen

10:48 pm Saturday 28 April 2001

(^^^^^ this is the time the game started)

Instead of ultimate, a few of us played disc golf at UH tonight. Katy, I thought of you as I kept score on my Handspring Visor.

Jerry was late; he walked the course backwards until he found us just after our tee off to Sandy In Defined Space.

AV Springfield is the name Marcel and I gave to the air vent beside the power plant. We named it after the Simpsons' hometown because of the power plant.

Will lost his disc on top of the Old Science Building, next to PGH in Entrance 14. We went inside, looking for a way to reach the roof, but found only locked doors. We waited while Will ran to his car and back to get a new disc.

Jerry made an incredible putt across the water to make a resounding thunk on the fountain-structure thing. Very nice.

Will made an incredible putt from the hedge under the tree to the Eternal Flame. Wow.

We finished the game around 1:30 am, talked in the parking lot until about 1:50am. I flipped a disc on the way home to see if I would come to work tonight to put this entry online. The disc was heads, and this is the entry.
