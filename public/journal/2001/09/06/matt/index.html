Matt

By Rob Nugen

11:25am Thursday 6 September 2001

Wow. I just read livejournal for the first time in a few days. Wow. wow.

So I'm thinkin' about how Colleen's entry saying "life just bitchslapped me in the face" is so true for me. How all the entries subsequent are supportive and shocked and how ... this is so real.

This is the kinda shocking news that happens in my dreams, from which I can wake up. But I'm not waking up from this one.

My 'grown up' life keeps me from just driving up to wherever Matt's living now, but I send mental Big Rob Hugs even so.

Nothing like a tragedy to pull people together.
