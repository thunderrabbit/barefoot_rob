arrrggh

By Rob Nugen

9:53am Wednesday 16 May 2001

Arrggh!

I reported to everyone that I could get into the building with a simple piece of cardboard, which is the technique I resorted to after I could not get a new electronic card after mine broke and our landlord refused to replace it because it still functioned, but wouldn't attach to my keys. That's the part that broke.

So I cut a hole in the card in an attempt to make it work on my keys, and I destroyed its functionality in the process.

The POINT is, that I am PISSED that I have to but a fucking new card because they wouldn't fucking replace the one that broke, not through neglect but through normal fucking usage and *AND* I didn't have to fucking tell them I could get into the building.I don't think it's appropriate for anyone but our landlord to get me a new fucking card.

10:27am

I talked with Karla who helped me understand this "problem" from different perspectives. Helped me realize that I'm just hurting myself with my anger.

I will pay the thirty fucking dollars for the card and I will devise a technique so amazing, so flawless that I will never lose the card, never allow it to be scratched or marred in any way. I will frame it elegantly as art when I leave the company, and when I'm old and give away all my possessions, I'll give away the frame and keep the card in my treasured scrapbook.
