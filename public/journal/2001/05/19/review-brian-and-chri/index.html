Rob Nugen #76

//+, //, //-, /+, /, - rec: playlist

2. // 7:29 Jakarta International Airport (Remix) groovin' quickly. Sampled car keys at the end.

 

from their movie score:

3. // 6:46 Ranchera Del Espacio ratatatatat with echoey strings and guitar

8. // 4:07 (untitled) drums and groovin' bass with brief guitar solos
