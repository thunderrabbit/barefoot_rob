Quick

By Rob Nugen

11:25am Sunday 06 May 2001

Oh I gotta write this.

I just called Philip Gayle after I emailed him to see if he could help me learn Japanese. His reply was on the lines of "absolutely! give me a call" instead of "um, yeah, um, but I'm busy busy" which is what I expected.

He invited me to join him and group of Japanese exchange students bowling this Thursday night, and told me briefly about the origin of this bowling night and mentioned that Alexis (that's Alexis) had been there last week.

"Oh my gosh that's the best way to get me anywhere is to say Alexis will be there!"

"Oh, you like Alexis?"

"Totally! And I can't even explain it! I could never date her, but I just totally LOVE her!! She's so awesome and so cool!!"

"Yeah, she's just a great person, very forward thinking and doing all sorts of great things.."

Philip continued, "oh, hey I just remembered I've got another call on the other line, so I should probably let you go. And it's a call from Japan..."

"Okay, see you Thursday!!"

So silly. This will be fun as anything!
