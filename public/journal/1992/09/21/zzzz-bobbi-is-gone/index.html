Bobbi is gone. Bobbi is in Chicago. Bobbi is in fucking Chicago. I've had a pissy day today and I think that's why. Fuck them all. I just want a new girl to kiss. I just want a new girl to kiss.

I JUST WANT A NEW GIRL TO KISS.
