By Rob Nugen

(transcribed from Book Two of my diary)

12:08 AM Tuesday 4 August 1992

“Today” I got a check from Nishika for $698.50.

RIGHT!!!

That is totally cool. I shall deposit it and $100 cash tomorrow. There is a totally beautiful girl visible in the next building. She has on a white long T shirt and was sitting at the table eating and reading. She’s blonde. I watched her for several minutes, but it seems she’s left the room for good. How do I meet her? Hmmm.
