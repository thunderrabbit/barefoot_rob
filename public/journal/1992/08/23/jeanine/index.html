23 Aug 1992, 00:00

Jeanine

By Rob Nugen

(transcribed from Book Two of my diary)

2:50 AM Sunday Aug 23 1992.

Molly & I still have not gotten a proper amount of sleep. Jeanine just called and I half asleep / half jokingly invited her over. She accepted. It will be nice to hug her, but I don't want to be awake. I imagine we'll go to House of Pies.

11:30PM called Laura.

11:59:45 PM

Jeanine got here today at 4:20AM. She talked & talked as we all 3 lay in bed. I cuddled with Jeanine and gave her kisses on her back and neck and ear. We didn't do anything thought. I slept util about 9AM & called Castle of Dreams. Then my hard drive on my computer fully died. Dead. Shit... It had been dying, and I have it all backed up, but it still sucks. At noon, I woke Jeanine up and she went "home" to the Towers, where she is currently staying with Imelda, her old suite mate, since she didn't get a room in the Quad.

Molly and I went to the Galleria and got a book bag for me. Then we picked up Jeanine & then went to Marcel's and picked up Marcel and we met Maggie, John, Danielle, and Charlie and saw Far and Away. It was a cool movie. (At the end of the night, I hugged Maggie and whispered in her ear a line from the movie, "Her tits are too big for her to be going to church.") After that, Molly took Jeanine home, and the rest of us waited at Sound Warehouse. Marcel, Charlie and I played "Shopping-Cart-Warrior Frisbee," where one dude throws the frisbee and the other 2 run like crazy and catch it in the carts. Very fun. Then we went to Marco's and at Mexican food. Now we are home. I just called Laura and she totally misses me. I think of her only in passing...
