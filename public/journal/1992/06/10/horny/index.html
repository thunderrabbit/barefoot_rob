By Rob Nugen

(transcribed from Book Two of my diary)

2:40 AM Wednesday June 10, 1992

Today I installed Turbo Pascal 6.0 and Turbo C 2.01 on the computer and ran stacker to make it all fit. I called Lady Di’s bulletin board and chatted with her for 2 hours about. Our chat turned to sex for a while and she hinted at being interested in having sex with me. I don’t know what will happen. She’s 45 and has a boyfriend. But we flirted and talked about her wearing a birthday suit to the Castle Super Soaker Party. She seemed Ok with that. I left a message on her board asking for a female roomate (on the board) and she said a girl named Velvet Box (!) would be interested in rooming with Thunder Rabbit. Cool. We’ll see what happens.
