yay SOGO

By Rob Nugen

14:28 Sunday 18 February 2018 JST

I was late to SOGO because I was late leaving and forgot my phone so had to come back.

I was a bit late arriving, but they were late starting so it all worked out. Haha we worked out as well. My toe injury did not bleed and was basically fine, though now it’s dirty. I was puffed by the group workout at the end, running while piggybacking Naoki, but even more impressive was him running while piggybacking me.

Went to lunch with Zach, Naoki, Ai, and Asuka at the mexican restaurant where the SOGO group has been going recently. Hella closer than the other Mexican place, but fewer lunch options.

Glad I got my phone so Tariq and I could coordinate meeting after SOGO.
