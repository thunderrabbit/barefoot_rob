Great hanging out with Mark

By Rob Nugen

06:11 Monday 12 November 2018 JST

Mark rode 4km from the 古民家公園 to our house yesterday to meet Lin and see Marble Track 3 in person. He had some great ideas for MT3:

  • Encourage teambuilding among youth by giving them a certain set of track pieces, tools, and time limit to make a track that fits a certain set of requirements.
  • Set my camera so the whole set is in focus.
  • Add narration or speech bubbles to explain the story in more detail.
