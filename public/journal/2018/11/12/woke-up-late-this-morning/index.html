woke up late this morning

By Rob Nugen

06:24 Monday 12 November 2018 JST

I usuually set my alarm for 5am on Mondays and Wednesdays so I can leave the house at 5:45am. This morning I turned off my 5am alarm and planned to snooze until my second alarm, but the phone was in silent mode so that alarm did not sound. Fortunately, Lin had set an alarm for 5:35. I had already packed my computer so I got out early enough to catch my preferred train, featuring enough seats that I can sit and write, or snooze, which I plan to do now.
