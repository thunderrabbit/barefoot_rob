travel resources and ROBS RUNES

By Rob Nugen

Tuesday 21 October 1997

Today at work I mostly read The Pleiadian Agenda, and “Communities” magazine. And I surfed the web, starting with www.ic.org Intentional Communities page.

In the past several weeks, I have been finding many diverse resources that will help with my trip!

  • Backpackers.com, designed to give travelers an email address and forum for communication and resources
  • American Youth Hostels, nonprofit organization dedicated to making travel possible for those on a budget through its low-cost, self-service network of accommodations
  • Intentional Communities, an organizational network thingy of communities around the country

And I’m excited!!!!

Today I started my book called ROB’S RUNES in which I intend to record any consultations I make with my runes.
