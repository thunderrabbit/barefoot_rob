catch up

By Rob Nugen

Okay.

It’s been a week.

The Techno Cosmic Mass was pretty cool, but not super hella crazy phat awesome like I had hoped!

Great pics though, and I scanned them and will put them up here soon!!!!! I promise!!

That Thursday I was at Ares Robotics at like 7:30am. Wes and I listened to Sarah on KTRU, and I called her a couple of times; she said she would be on the air until 9:00pm, which was funny cause she had just said, “I’m so awake!”

I took four pictures of the altars before any work was done on them that day:

Earth Air Water Fire

The top blue thing was a shower curtain. Here it is tacked on, with the water fountain squirting. Our mountain is visible in the foreground.

Drove everything up there to the George R. Brown and began to put it all together.

Earth Air Water Fire

In the center of the room was a big structure. Here is Wes on it.
Here is Wes under it. Here it is being decorated.

A video crew recorded the event. They didn’t record me decorated.

Dion, worked on the entrance, while Marisol showed off sleepy Akira

The event started at 7pm. I took pictures of all four altars. Hmm. I thought I did, but I musta only scanned two.

Earth Water

I loved my water altar! I took an artsy pic of it.

There was a gospel singer and a rapper, and dancers and all kinds of fun.

Some funny person put a bar of soap in the water altar!
The funniest thing is that someone had a bar of soap at the event!

Dion took this picture of me after the event. My fingers were wrapped up after I reinjured an old injury.

Afterward, we packed up the good stuff, threw away the rest and went home.
