technology

By Rob Nugen

12:15pm Tuesday 2 March 1999

Technology is pretty amazin’. Dude and I have been chatting over ICQ and working on a new website. Free of charge (given the computer and phone line) we have used a geocities website for 11 megs of space, an iname email address, and a listbot email list for this new website (for a non profit organization that can’t really afford anything).

But I’m just marveling that all this is like free and just out there to be used, and I’m sure there’s tons more that we simply haven’t seen yet.

Plus, chatting over ICQ our two apartments, which are physically 2+ miles apart, are suddenly virtually side by side in the MR Laboratories virtual office.

I’m going to actually go outside now and help Wende move.
