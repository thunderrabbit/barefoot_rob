lry chuck e cheese 2

By Rob Nugen

Chuck E. Cheese

Wednesday 6 May 1998

Dinner this night was at Chuck E. Cheese's and we had 14 youth show up! That's the most we've had at any Wednesday night dinner. We had representation from 3 or 4 churches!

A great time was had by all, I believe. I mostly didn't spend many tokens. Probably about 1 dollar worth and then I just watched the youth doing various games and stuff.

Paul did super great on Solar Attack (or something like that) big video screen video game, getting past any level I had seen on it.

We had super great fun in the ball pit, with about 10 of us in there, throwing the balls all over the place (mostly inside the maze of tubes) and having a chaotic wonderful time.

After a couple of hours, parents wandered back to pick up their respective youth and we all left.
