State of My Life Address

7 July 1998

State of My Life

  1. Wow! so so so so so MUCH has happened!

  2. I quit my job. I mentioned that already.

  3. I'm busier now that I quit my job than I was before!

  4. I got a flat tire on the way to the dentist. That was an interesting story which just completed today.

  5. I went to Rochester, New York for one week for a huge conference called General Assembly! (GA) It was amazing.

  6. At GA, I decided to re-start The Feel Good Net

  7. At GA, I met Leif Hardison, and soon after GA, we got yruu.org set up.

  8. After GA, Wende and two friends we met there drove to Niagra Falls. Parked on the US side and walked to the Canadian side. Amazing.

  9. On our flight home from GA, the pilot swung by Niagra Falls, giving us a spectacular view from only 4000 feet in the air. Truly awe inspiring.

  10. Just today I signed up for health insurance as a self employed type person.

  11. Also just today I signed up as a FlashNet distributor. If you want a dialup ISP, gimme a call!

  12. I designed new business cards for myself. They are cool because they're hand written.

  13. This morning, I called in to the radio station and won four tickets to IMAX. But you'd never guess how unless you heard me!

  14. Look for all these stories and more coming soon to my journal entries!
