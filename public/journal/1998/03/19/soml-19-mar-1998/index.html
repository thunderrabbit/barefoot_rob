State of My Life

19:44 Thursday 19 March 1998

STATE OF MY LIFE

  1. I normally don't do State of My Life Addresses so chronologically close together, but I am now.
  2. At work I've been working much longer hours than normal and I'm still not getting my project done as fast as I want. I'm working in C++ and I'm stuck on a problem I'm having with some objects written by another group. The objects are designed to create a menu system and allow user input, etcetera. However, for some reason I can't get them to trap CTRL-Z the way I want. It's been pissin' me off for 4 days now.
  3. I just found out about dates that Tori Amos is going to play on her tour! I'm way excited! I'd like to see her in Los Angeles, because then I can see Jen, who is my Tori Amos fan soulmate! Jen's birthday is the same as mine (25 March).
  4. I'm listening to nine inch nails closer to god CD.
  5. This weekend I'm going to Austin to play Ultimate!
