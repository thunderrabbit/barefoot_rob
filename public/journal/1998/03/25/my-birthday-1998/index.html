my birthday 1998

By Rob Nugen

My 28th birthday!

25 March 1998

Today I was accepted into The Spirit Ring and I have spruced up my page a bit in hopes that more people will come see my site. I had 710 hits on my japanese counter and 318 hits on my other counter... We'll see what happens...

At work I found a quarter on my desk with a note written on the back of one of my business cards: 

       Happy B-day.
         This is for the 
          M&M Machine  
              - Molly

I bought a small handful of M&Ms before I sat down.

Since then I haven't actually worked yet today. I've just been goofin' around on my page and stuff.
