The Sculptor in the Sky — Teal Scott. Paperback.

A spirituality / self-development book on consciousness, creating one's reality, and finding meaning. Cosmic cover art: cupped hands cradling a glowing Earth against a starfield.

**Why a new owner would be happy**

- A good story to tell: “Teal Scott” is the earlier name of Teal Swan, the well-known (and polarizing) spiritual teacher — an early book under the former name carries collector/curiosity interest.
- An approachable entry into her work for anyone drawn to consciousness / manifestation themes.
- Paperback; light shelf wear (a corner crease), interior sound.
