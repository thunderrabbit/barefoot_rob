The Cosmic War: Interplanetary Warfare, Modern Physics and Ancient Texts — Joseph P. Farrell. Adventures Unlimited Press. ISBN 978-1-931882-75-0.

Alternative-history / fringe-physics. Farrell argues the “exploded planet hypothesis” (the asteroid belt as the remains of a destroyed world) and reads ancient myths — Sumerian, Egyptian, Greek, and from the Americas — as records of a literal ancient interplanetary war, bringing in plasma cosmology and “scalar physics.”
