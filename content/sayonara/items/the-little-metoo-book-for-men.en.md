The Little #MeToo Book for Men — Mark Greene. ThinkPlay Partners (NYC). ISBN 978-1-7294-7389-4.

A concise (~75 pages), no-holds-barred call to action examining how “man box culture” harms men and women, inviting men out of silence and isolation. Greene is a Good Men Project senior editor who writes on men's issues for Salon, Yes! Magazine, the BBC and the New York Times, and is the author of Remaking Manhood.
