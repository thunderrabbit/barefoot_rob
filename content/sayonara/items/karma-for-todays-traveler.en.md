Karma for Today's Traveler — Phra Bhasakorn Bhavilai, with David Freyer. Phra Borommathat Doi Suthep Foundation, Chiang Mai. ISBN 974-656-846-9.

An accessible look at the Buddhist concept of cause and effect — mind, intention, and the creative power of human action — written for a modern, traveling audience. The author studied physics and was a successful photographer before becoming a Theravada monk. A lovely cover painting of a lantern-lit temple procession (Doi Suthep).

**Why a new owner would be happy**

- A gentle, readable entry into karma and Buddhist thought — no background needed.
- Strong Thailand / Chiang Mai provenance; a nice traveler's keepsake.
- Good condition (still carries a ฿100 shop sticker — part of its travel story).
